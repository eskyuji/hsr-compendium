# Honkai: Star Rail — V2.X Lore Compendium: Overview

## Summary

Covers v2.0–2.7, the Penacony arc: a resort world running on a shared Stellaron-powered Dreamscape, whose investigation exposes Sunday's scheme to remake it and the Family's role in its founding, followed by a string of shorter interludes before departure for Amphoreus.

This continues the compendium begun in `00-overview.md` (which covers v1.0–1.6, the Herta Space Station / Jarilo-VI / Xianzhou Luofu arcs). This document covers v2.0 through v2.7 — the **Penacony** arc and the interludes that follow it, ending with the Astral Express's departure for Amphoreus (v3.0, outside this compendium's scope).

## The throughline: dreams, Order, and free will

Where v1.x was structured around three Stellaron crises, v2.x is built around a single sustained mystery: Penacony, "the Planet of Festivities," runs almost entirely on a shared, IPC-seeded Stellaron-powered Dreamscape. The arc interrogates what a society built on curated dreams costs its people, and introduces the Aeon **Xipe** (Harmony) and the enigmatic Aeon **Ena** (Order) as major cosmological forces — expanding the setting's mythology considerably beyond the Destruction/Preservation/Hunt/Abundance concepts introduced in v1.x. See [`characters/detail/aeons-reference.md`](characters/detail/aeons-reference.md) for both.
