# Honkai: Star Rail — V4.X Lore Compendium: Overview

## Summary

Covers v4.0–4.4 (ongoing): the Planarcadia arc, presided over by Aha, Aeon of Elation — opening as celebrity spectacle around the Phantasmoon Games before pivoting into a darker mystery around navigator Himeko's hidden past and a Lord Ravager posing as her father.

This continues the compendium from `00-overview.md` (v1.x), `01-overview-v2.md` (v2.x, Penacony), and `02-overview-v3.md` (v3.x, Amphoreus). This document covers v4.0 through the current patch, **v4.4** (released July 15, 2026) — the **Planarcadia** arc.

**This arc is still ongoing.** Unlike the earlier compendium entries, which cover fully resolved story arcs, Planarcadia is the game's live, unfinished main story as of this writing. Treat everything below as current-best-understanding rather than a settled account — later patches may recontextualize or outright overturn details here, the way v3.8 overturned assumptions about Stellarons that had stood since v1.x.

## The throughline: elation, spectacle, and a hidden cost

Planarcadia is presided over by **Aha**, the Aeon of Elation (see [`characters/detail/aeons-reference.md`](characters/detail/aeons-reference.md)) — introducing the setting's **Path of Elation** to both the story and the gameplay systems. Where Amphoreus (v3.x) was apocalyptic and mythic in tone, Planarcadia opens as a theme-park-like world of celebrity welcomes, games, and spectacle. Multiple sources note this tonal shift is likely intentional scaffolding: an upbeat surface built to set up a darker turn as the arc progresses, similar in structure to how Penacony's "Land of Dreams" veneer concealed real harm. By v4.3–4.4, that darker turn has clearly arrived, centered on a long-hidden secret in navigator **[Himeko](characters/detail/himeko.md)**'s past and the reappearance of a **Lord Ravager**, continuing the recurring v1.x–v3.x pattern of Nanook's agents lurking behind each world's crisis.

## Key throughline characters

**[Himeko](characters/detail/himeko.md)** (navigator of the Astral Express since the v1.x prologue) becomes the arc's emotional center from v4.3 onward. **[Asat Pramad](characters/detail/asat-pramad.md)**, a Lord Ravager of the Antimatter Legion, is the arc's primary antagonist to date. New Planarcadia figures include **[Yao Guang](characters/detail/yao-guang.md)**, **[Sparxie](characters/detail/sparxie.md)**, **[Evanescia](characters/detail/evanescia.md)**, and **[Ashveil](characters/detail/ashveil.md)**. See the faction and character docs below for details.

Note: v4.2's anniversary content included a crossover collaboration event with the anime studio MAPPA ("Death in the Afternoon," featuring guest characters). Collaboration/crossover content is not part of the core Honkai: Star Rail continuity and is intentionally omitted from this lore compendium.
