# Honkai: Star Rail Lore Compendium

A fan-made lore compendium for **Honkai: Star Rail**, covering the story arcs, factions, and characters from the game's launch (v1.0) through the ongoing Planarcadia arc (v4.x). It's organized into four layers — era overviews, story arcs (each with a prose overview and a beat-by-beat timeline), faction write-ups, and character profiles — all cross-linked so you can jump from a faction to its members, from a character to their allies and affiliations, or from an arc to the people and organizations involved.

**Full index: [`00-index.md`](00-index.md)** — the complete table of contents with links to every document in the compendium.

## Reading order for a newcomer

1. Start with `00-overview.md` through `03-overview-v4.md` for the big picture.
2. Read the arc overviews in `arcs/` in order for the story; dip into `arcs/detail/` for specifics.
3. Use the faction and character sections above as a reference while reading — click through to `detail/` whenever a name needs more context.
