## The throughline: a simulated world stuck in a loop

Amphoreus is structurally different from every world visited in v1.x and v2.x: it isn't a "real" planet in the same sense, but a virtual, data-and-memoria-based simulation running inside a discarded artifact called **Scepter δ-me13** (created as part of the divine corpus of **Nous**, an Aeon associated with intelligence/erudition-adjacent concepts — see [`characters/detail/aeons-reference.md`](characters/detail/aeons-reference.md)). The simulation has run through tens of millions of cycles, each ending in apocalyptic destruction, as a way of gathering data. The arc's core mystery is: who is running this experiment, why, and can its self-aware inhabitants ever actually break free of the loop.

This also marks the point where the Astral Express's method of travel changes — Amphoreus is reached via Mirror technology from the "[Garden of Recollection](factions/detail/garden-of-recollection.md)," tied to the crew's broader mission (introduced across v2.x and v3.x) to "reveal the truth behind the Three Paths."
