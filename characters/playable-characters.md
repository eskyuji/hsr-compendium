# Playable Characters (v1.x)

## Summary

The v1.x playable roster: the Astral Express crew (Trailblazer, March 7th, Dan Heng, Himeko, Welt Yang) plus the characters introduced across Herta Space Station, Jarilo-VI/Belobog, and the Xianzhou Luofu.

Grouped by the arc/faction where they're introduced. Descriptions focus on story role and identity, not gameplay mechanics/builds. Names link to their full profile in `detail/` where one exists.

## Astral Express Crew

- **[Trailblazer](detail/trailblazer.md)** — the protagonist; bonded with a Stellaron in the prologue, exists in Destruction and Preservation "Path" variants depending on story context.
- **[March 7th](detail/march-7th.md)** — an amnesiac girl found frozen in ice; cheerful and determined to find out who she was.
- **[Dan Heng](detail/dan-heng.md)** — reserved Astral Express archivist and exile from the Xianzhou Luofu; secretly connected to the legendary Imbibitor Lunae, Dan Feng (revealed in the Luofu arc).
- **[Himeko](detail/himeko.md)** — the Express's navigator and de facto elder-sister figure to the crew.
- **[Welt Yang](detail/welt-yang.md)** — a powerful, philosophical passenger with a mysterious past tied to Destruction.

## Introduced on Herta Space Station

- **[Herta](detail/herta.md)** — the eccentric, reclusive genius who founded and directs the Herta Space Station (usually appears via a small proxy doll rather than in person).
- **Asta** — Lead Researcher at Herta Space Station; coordinates the station's defense during the Antimatter Legion attack.
- **Arlan** — a hardened Herta Space Station security officer who joins the fight against the Legion.

## Introduced on Jarilo-VI / Belobog

- **[Bronya](detail/bronya.md)** — Cocolia's adopted daughter; torn between loyalty and conscience, later becomes Belobog's new Supreme Guardian.
- **[Seele](detail/seele.md)** — an Underground fighter whose Quantum ability lets her access a dangerous, more powerful alternate self.
- **[Gepard Landau](detail/gepard.md)** — an honorable Silvermane Guard captain who ultimately stands with the Trailblazer against Cocolia.
- **[Clara](detail/clara.md)** — a gentle young girl who pilots the towering guardian robot Svarog.
- **[Natasha](detail/natasha.md)** — the Underground's doctor and the leader of Wildfire.
- **[Serval](detail/serval.md)** — a rock-and-roll-loving Architect-engineer who questions Cocolia's authoritarian turn.
- **[Sampo Koski](detail/sampo.md)** — a charismatic con artist and smuggler who proves a valuable, if unreliable, ally.
- **[Pela](detail/pela.md)** — a sharp, no-nonsense Silvermane Guard intelligence officer.
- **Hook** — a scrappy young member of the Underground's "Wildfire Youth" group.

## Introduced on the Xianzhou Luofu

- **[Jing Yuan](detail/jing-yuan.md)** — the Luofu's Arbiter-General, a laid-back but formidable strategist bonded to a "Lightning-Lord" spiritus.
- **[Yanqing](detail/yanqing.md)** — Jing Yuan's eager, sword-obsessed young disciple.
- **[Fu Xuan](detail/fu-xuan.md)** — Master Diviner of the Luofu's Divination Commission, able to foresee danger to those she protects.
- **[Bailu](detail/bailu.md)** — a young Vidyadhara physician-in-training with genuine healing/resurrection abilities.
- **[Luocha](detail/luocha.md)** — a mysterious, elegant traveling merchant-healer with hidden depths.
- **[Tingyun](detail/tingyun-phantylia.md)** — a warm, helpful Luofu merchant who accompanies the party — secretly the Lord Ravager Phantylia.
- **[Blade](detail/blade.md)** — a nearly indestructible, self-destructive swordsman and Stellaron Hunter with deep ties to the Luofu's past.
- **[Qingque](detail/qingque.md)** — a mahjong-obsessed, laid-back Cloud Knight who fights via a chaotic bamboo-tile ability.
- **[Sushang](detail/sushang.md)** — an earnest young swordswoman training to become a Cloud Knight.
- **[Xueyi](detail/xueyi.md)** — an enforcer of the Ten-Lords Commission who judges the guilty.
- **Hanya** — a by-the-book clerk of the Ten-Lords Commission.
- **Huohuo** — a timid Cloud Knight recruit drawn into the Heliobi ghost-hunting side stories (later arc content).
- **Lynx** — a Vidyadhara medic from the Xianzhou's frontier settlements.
- **[Imbibitor Lunae](detail/dan-heng.md)** — Dan Heng's ancient, true draconic form/identity, unlocked during the climax of the Luofu arc.

## Introduced via the Stellaron Hunters / IPC (recurring across arcs)

- **[Kafka](detail/kafka.md)** — an elegant, calculating Stellaron Hunter who sets the plot in motion in the prologue.
- **[Silver Wolf](detail/silver-wolf.md)** — a teenage hacker Stellaron Hunter with reality-bending digital skills.
- **[Topaz](detail/topaz.md) (& Numby)** — an IPC "Stoneheart" debt specialist introduced at the end of the Jarilo-VI arc.

*Note: exact gacha banner release order within v1.0–1.6 is not tracked in this document; characters are grouped by story/faction context instead, which is more stable than patch-specific banner scheduling.
