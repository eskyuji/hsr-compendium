# Playable Characters (v4.0–4.4, ongoing)

## Summary

The v4.x playable roster so far: new Planarcadia natives (Yao Guang, Sparxie, Evanescia, Ashveil) aligned with the Path of Elation, plus expanded roles for returning crew members Silver Wolf and Himeko as the arc's central mystery unfolds.

Grouped by introduction context. Descriptions focus on story identity, not gameplay mechanics/builds. As this arc is still unfolding, some characters' full story roles are not yet clear. Names link to their full profile in `detail/` where one exists.

## Introduced in the main Planarcadia arc

- **[Yao Guang](detail/yao-guang.md)** — a Planarcadia-native support figure aligned with the Path of Elation, introduced in v4.0.
- **[Sparxie](detail/sparxie.md)** — introduced in v4.0's second phase; closely tied to Yao Guang and to Sparkle (introduced in the v1.x/v2.x era) within Planarcadia's Elation-aligned cast.
- **[Evanescia](detail/evanescia.md)** — introduced in v4.2 as a "Special Observer" figure connected to the Phantasmoon Games' spectacle-driven systems.
- **[Ashveil](detail/ashveil.md)** — introduced in v4.1, an execute-focused Hunt-path fighter.

## Returning characters with an expanded role

- **[Silver Wolf](detail/silver-wolf.md)** — the Stellaron Hunter hacker first introduced in the v1.0 prologue (see v1.x compendium); reappears in v4.2 with an expanded showcase of her reality/game-manipulation abilities, fitting naturally into Planarcadia's game-show-like setting.
- **[Himeko](detail/himeko.md)** — the Astral Express's navigator since the v1.0 prologue (see v1.x compendium); becomes the emotional and narrative center of the arc from v4.3 onward as her hidden past comes under scrutiny. A "Nova Himeko" identity/form is introduced in v4.4, tied directly to the arc's unresolved revelations about her true nature.

## Note on collaboration content

v4.2's anniversary period included a crossover collaboration with the anime studio MAPPA ("Death in the Afternoon"), which added guest characters from outside the core Honkai: Star Rail continuity. These are gameplay-only crossover content and are not included in this lore compendium.

*Note: exact gacha banner release order and full character rosters for v4.0–4.4 are less settled in available sources than for earlier, completed arcs, since this arc is still current. Expect this document to need revision as later patches clarify remaining details. Sparkle does not yet have a dedicated `characters/detail/` profile.*
