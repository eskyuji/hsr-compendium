# Major NPCs and Antagonists (v4.0–4.4, ongoing)

## Summary

The v4.x antagonist/NPC roster centers on Asat Pramad, the Lord Ravager posing as Himeko's father, and Aha, the Aeon of Elation who orchestrates the Phantasmoon Games — with Himeko's true nature and "the Voracity" left unresolved as of v4.4.

Names link to their full profile in `detail/` where one exists; Aeons link to the consolidated `detail/aeons-reference.md`.

## [Asat Pramad](detail/asat-pramad.md)

A **Lord Ravager of the Antimatter Legion and Emanator of Nanook**, the Aeon of Destruction — placing him alongside Phantylia (v1.x, Xianzhou Luofu) and Irontomb (v3.x, Amphoreus) in the series' recurring roster of Destruction's agents. Rather than direct military conquest, he favors long-term psychological and strategic manipulation, reportedly spending up to a century positioning his forces for a single symbolic gesture on other worlds, and marking conquered planets with a mocking "smiling face" imprint. On Planarcadia, he spent years posing as navigator Himeko's father, using that position of trust to unravel the impossibilities in her official history (her survival of the Withering Curse, her Phantasmoon Games victory, her restoration of the Astral Express) before being defeated in the v4.4 confrontation. Whether he is fully eliminated or merely set back is not yet clear as of this writing.

## [Aha](detail/aeons-reference.md)

The Aeon of Elation, who presides directly over Planarcadia and personally orchestrates the Phantasmoon Games, including the decision (v4.2) to raise the Games' ultimate prize to permanent Aeon-hood. Aha's own motives remain largely unexplored as of v4.4.

## Fulwish

Leader of the Research Society of Happy Smiles; publicly challenges the wisdom of the Phantasmoon Games' escalated stakes in v4.2, representing institutional skepticism within Planarcadia toward Aha's game.

## [Himeko](detail/himeko.md) (expanded role)

Originally introduced as a playable Astral Express crew member in the v1.0 prologue (see v1.x compendium), Himeko's backstory becomes the arc's central mystery from v4.3 onward. Her official history — the Withering Curse, an earlier Phantasmoon Games victory, and her restoration of the abandoned Astral Express — is thrown into question by Asat Pramad, culminating in the "Nova Himeko" revelations of v4.4. Full clarity on what this means for her identity going forward is not yet available.

## The Voracity

A force or entity that "awakens" during the climax of v4.4's central confrontation; its precise nature is not yet clarified in available sources as of this writing, and its role is expected to be developed further in upcoming patches.
