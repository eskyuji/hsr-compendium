# Jing Yuan

## Summary

Jing Yuan is the Xianzhou Luofu's Arbiter-General, commanding the Cloud Knights and bearing primary responsibility for the ship's defense; he becomes one of the Trailblazer's steadfast allies during the Luofu arc after recruiting them under the pretext of hunting the Stellaron Hunter Kafka. His arc reveals a long, complicated history tied to the Xianzhou's past (recognizing Dan Heng's true identity as Dan Feng/Imbibitor Lunae) and a mentorship role toward his retainer Yanqing, alongside a close strategic partnership with Fu Xuan. No Path/Aeon misattributions were found in this entry — Jing Yuan and the Cloud Knights are correctly framed as a Xianzhou Luofu military faction with no stated Aeon allegiance.

## Overview

The Xianzhou Luofu's Arbiter-General — a laid-back, formidable strategist bonded to a "Lightning-Lord" spiritus, and one of the Trailblazer's most steadfast allies once the Stellaron crisis begins.

## History

As the Luofu's Arbiter-General, Jing Yuan commands its [Cloud Knights](../../factions/detail/cloud-knights.md) and holds primary responsibility for the ship's defense. He enlists the Trailblazer's help early in the Luofu arc under the pretext of capturing **[Kafka](kafka.md)**, while privately suspecting a deeper crisis is unfolding — a suspicion that proves well-founded once the Ambrosial Arbor's corruption and Phantylia's sabotage come to light.

Jing Yuan personally recognizes **[Dan Heng](dan-heng.md)**'s true identity the moment he transforms into Imbibitor Lunae, addressing him as "an old friend" and invoking the name Dan Feng — establishing that Jing Yuan's own history with the Xianzhou stretches back further than his current post suggests. In the arc's climax, threatened with being overtaken by Mara/Antimatter corruption himself, Jing Yuan asks Dan Heng to strike him down rather than risk becoming a danger to his own people — a request resolved without his death, but one that underscores both his trust in Dan Heng and the genuine stakes of the Mara affliction. He later relies on **[Fu Xuan](fu-xuan.md)**'s unconventional strategic gambit (summoning the legendary Reignbow via the Cloudpeer Telescope) during a separate military crisis, choosing to trust her judgment over more conventional caution.

## Personality

Easygoing and unhurried in manner, Jing Yuan's calm is deceptive — he's an exceptionally capable strategist and fighter whose relaxed demeanor conceals both deep experience and real emotional weight from his own past. He mentors **[Yanqing](yanqing.md)** with a deliberate mix of encouragement and restraint, believing the young prodigy needs to learn "the ability and experience of curbing that strength" as much as raw skill.

## Related figures

**Yanqing** (his retainer and de facto adopted protégé), **Fu Xuan** (Master Diviner and trusted strategic partner), **Dan Heng/Imbibitor Lunae** (an old, complicated connection from the Xianzhou's past), **Baiheng** (referenced in his backstory as a significant past companion tied to the Xianzhou's older history).
