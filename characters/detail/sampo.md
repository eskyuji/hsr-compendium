# Sampo Koski

## Summary

Sampo Koski is a Belobog Underground fixer, con artist, and smuggler with a reputation for scams and unreliability that masks a genuine, consistent loyalty to the people he cares about. Throughout the Jarilo-VI arc he proves a valuable ally to the Trailblazer's crew and to Wildfire, repeatedly delivering useful information, supplies, or timely help whenever it truly matters. He operates as an independent figure loosely tied to Wildfire and the Underground community rather than as a formal member of either.

## Overview

A charming, mustachioed con artist and smuggler whose surface-level unreliability hides a surprisingly consistent loyalty to the people who matter to him.

## History

A fixture of Belobog's Underground scene, Sampo makes his living on schemes, scams, and smuggling — the kind of character other Underground residents are quick to warn newcomers about. Despite this reputation, he proves a genuinely valuable ally to the Trailblazer's crew and to **[Wildfire](../../factions/detail/wildfire.md)** throughout the Jarilo-VI arc, repeatedly showing up with useful information, supplies, or timely assistance whenever the stakes actually matter, even if he grumbles about it the entire time.

## Personality

Glib, self-interested on the surface, and always working an angle — but with a soft spot for the Underground community he technically preys on. Sampo's arc across the Belobog story is less about redemption than about revealing that the con-artist routine was always partly a performance covering real loyalty underneath.

## Faction

Independent operator, loosely aligned with Wildfire and the Underground community. See [`factions/belobog-factions.md`](../../factions/belobog-factions.md).
