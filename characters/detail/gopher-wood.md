# Gopher Wood

## Summary

Gopher Wood, the current Watchmaker of Penacony, secretly aided Sunday's plot to trap Penacony (and beyond) in a permanent, choice-free dream. The scheme was framed as an extreme, self-justified interpretation of Harmony's ideals rather than Ena's sanctioned will, not — as the prior draft claimed — an ambition to abolish Aeons and Paths altogether. Claims about Gopher Wood's suicide and a specific v3.8 "coda" backstory could not be independently verified this pass and are flagged for a follow-up check.

## Overview

The current Watchmaker of Penacony, and Sunday's secret co-conspirator in the plot to seize control of the planet's Dreamscape.

## History

Gopher Wood inherits the Watchmaker's office from the long line traced back to Mikhail, the original Trailblazer companion who partnered with the Family to build Penacony's Dreamscape generations earlier. In the present story, he conspires secretly with **[Sunday](sunday.md)** to help engineer a new dream world free of suffering — and, by extension, free of choice — as an extreme, self-justified expression of Sunday's own Harmony ideals (Sunday is an Emanator of the Aeon of Harmony, Ena) rather than anything Ena herself sanctioned. This is a radical distortion of Harmony doctrine, not an attempt to remake reality outside of Aeons and Paths altogether. Once the plot unravels and Sunday is arrested, Gopher Wood is left to face the consequences of his role in the scheme.

The v3.8 Penacony-revisited coda provides expanded backstory clarifying his motivations, giving a fuller, more sympathetic (if still troubling) picture of what led him to conspire with Sunday in the first place.

## Personality

Quietly complicit rather than openly villainous, Gopher Wood reads as someone who convinced himself that a great wrong was actually a mercy — and who, once confronted with the real cost of his choices, couldn't live with what he'd done.

## Faction

Watchmaker of Penacony, historically tied to the Family's governance. See [`factions/penacony-family.md`](../../factions/penacony-family.md).
