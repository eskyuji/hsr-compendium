# Ashveil

## Summary

Ashveil is a showman-styled Hunt-path playable character introduced in Honkai: Star Rail v4.1, whose flashy public persona fits Planarcadia's theme-park atmosphere. His kit centers on marking enemies as "Bait" to concentrate damage and providing team-wide support through execution-focused tactics. A possible identity as La Mancha, leader of the Galaxy Rangers, is noted but treated as speculative rather than confirmed, given how recently this character was introduced and the limited information available at time of writing.

## Overview

A showman-styled Hunt-path fighter introduced in v4.1, whose flashy public persona may conceal a much more significant identity: leadership of the Galaxy Rangers.

## History

Ashveil operates on the Path of the Hunt with a randomized, spectacle-driven combat style fitting for Planarcadia's theme-park atmosphere — marking enemies as "Bait" to concentrate damage and providing team-wide support through execution-focused tactics. Some sources connect him to **La Mancha**, the established leader of the [Galaxy Rangers](../../factions/detail/galaxy-rangers.md) (the organization [Boothill](boothill.md) uses as cover during his IPC vendetta in the v2.x era), though this connection is treated as more speculative than confirmed as of this writing.

## Personality

Theatrical and confident, with a showman's flair that fits naturally into Planarcadia's culture of performance and public attention, whatever quieter agenda might sit underneath.

## Faction

Possibly connected to the [Galaxy Rangers](../../factions/detail/galaxy-rangers.md).
