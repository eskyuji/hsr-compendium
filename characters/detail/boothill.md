# Boothill

## Summary

Boothill is a cyborg bounty hunter whose entire home community was destroyed by an IPC operation under director Oswaldo Schneider, leaving him the sole driving force behind a decades-long personal vendetta. He uses his Galaxy Rangers credentials purely as cover for tracking down IPC operatives — and Schneider specifically — across the galaxy. His backstory is one of the compendium's clearest illustrations of the IPC's "pragmatic" corporate reputation curdling into outright atrocity. His combat Path (Destruction) is a gameplay classification only and should not be read as a narrative allegiance to the Nanook or any Aeon; his actual faction ties are to the Galaxy Rangers (nominally) and, functionally, to no one but himself.

## Overview

A cyborg bounty hunter driven by a decades-long vendetta against the IPC — one of the clearest examples in the series of the corporation's "pragmatic" reputation curdling into outright atrocity.

## History

Boothill was found as an infant on the plains of Aeragan-Epharshel and raised by Graey and Nick alongside other children, learning ranching, horsemanship, and hunting in a pastoral frontier community. His birth name, translating to "loaded gun" in the local ancient language, proved grimly prophetic.

The IPC's Marketing Development Department, under director **Oswaldo Schneider**, invaded his homeworld to extract rare ore, ignoring local protest. When Boothill tried to intervene, the IPC destroyed his entire community — including his adoptive parents and a young child he had been personally caring for — in an act of outright genocide. Severely wounded and consumed by the need for revenge, he underwent extensive cyberization at the hands of a skilled but gruff doctor, emerging with mechanical limbs, enhanced capabilities, and a sharpened, synthetic edge to match his new purpose. He describes himself as having been "dead for a long time" — the cyberization as much a symbolic rebirth as a physical one.

He now operates under cover of the **[Galaxy Rangers](../../factions/detail/galaxy-rangers.md)**, using the organization's legitimacy as camouflage for his real mission: hunting down IPC operatives, and Oswaldo Schneider specifically, across multiple star systems, racking up an enormous bounty in the process.

## Personality

Loud, brash, and "extremely optimistic and unrestrained" in a way that masks genuine, focused trauma underneath — Boothill's cowboy showmanship is entertainment for everyone around him, but his actual mission is deadly serious.

## Faction

Nominally Galaxy Rangers; functionally an independent vigilante against the [IPC](../../factions/interastral-peace-corporation.md). Note: his in-game combat Path is Destruction, which is a gameplay/combat classification and carries no narrative implication of allegiance to the Nanook or any other Aeon.
