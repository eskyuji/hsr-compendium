# Tingyun (Phantylia)

## Summary

Tingyun is a trusted Luofu companion of the Trailblazer's crew who is ultimately revealed to be Phantylia, one of Nanook's Lord Ravagers, working to corrupt the Xianzhou Luofu from within via the Ambrosial Arbor. Unlike the Stellaron Hunters (who serve the Path of Finality and are not aligned with Destruction), Phantylia is a genuine, direct agent of Nanook, the Aeon of Destruction. She is defeated and banished after a climactic battle, and the crew mourns the "Tingyun" persona despite the betrayal.

## Overview

A warm, endlessly helpful Luofu merchant who travels with the Trailblazer's crew for most of the Xianzhou Luofu arc — and is ultimately revealed to be Phantylia, one of the seven Lord Ravagers serving Nanook, the Aeon of Destruction.

## History as Tingyun

For the bulk of the arc, Tingyun functions as a trusted, likable companion: resourceful, quick with connections and information, and seemingly as invested in the crew's success as anyone else aboard. This is, in retrospect, an elaborate and sustained deception — one of the most effective long-con reveals in the series.

## Revelation as Phantylia

As the Stellaron crisis in the Ambrosial Arbor escalates, Tingyun is revealed to be Phantylia in disguise, breaking her human guise entirely (in a striking, violent transformation) to pursue the Arbor's corrupted roots directly. As Phantylia, she commands multiple monstrous forms powered by the corrupted tree and fights the Trailblazer's party directly at the Scalegorge Waterscape and the hidden underwater city beneath the Luofu. She is ultimately defeated and banished, but not before delivering an ominous warning: "the Destruction of the Xianzhou is nigh."

## Aftermath

Despite the deception, the crew holds a small funeral to honor the "Tingyun" persona they had genuinely come to care for — an acknowledgment that the relationship, even if built on a lie of identity, produced real bonds along the way. This complicated grief is one of the more emotionally layered beats of the Luofu arc. The real Tingyun survives the ordeal and is later revived as **[Fugue](fugue.md)**.

## Faction

A Lord Ravager in direct service to Nanook, the Aeon of Destruction. Despite superficial resemblances in method, she is not aligned with the Stellaron Hunters, who are tied to the Path of Finality and are ultimately working against Nanook's Path rather than for it. See [`factions/detail/lord-ravagers.md`](../../factions/detail/lord-ravagers.md) and [`factions/stellaron-hunters.md`](../../factions/stellaron-hunters.md).
