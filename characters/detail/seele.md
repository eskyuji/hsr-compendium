# Seele

## Summary

Seele is a Quantum-elemental fighter from Belobog's Underground who holds a prominent role in Wildfire, the Underground's resistance and mutual-aid movement led by Natasha. Shaped by scarcity and hardship in the Underworld, she operates independently but becomes a key ally to the Trailblazer's crew once they take refuge underground. The document's claims about her faction (Wildfire, not any Aeon-aligned group) and combat element (Quantum, a Path-agnostic combat type, not a narrative Aeon allegiance) are consistent with established lore and required no correction.

## Overview

An Underground Belobog fighter whose Quantum ability lets her access a more powerful, more dangerous alternate self — a reflection, in gameplay and story alike, of a life defined by scarcity and the need to protect what little she has.

## History

Seele grew up in Belobog's Underworld, a harsh environment of food scarcity and daily survival where the surface city's Supreme Guardian's neglect is felt most acutely. She holds a prominent position within **[Wildfire](../../factions/detail/wildfire.md)**, the Underground's resistance and mutual-aid movement led by [Natasha](natasha.md), and becomes one of the Trailblazer's key allies once the crew flees into the Underground after being framed on the surface.

## Personality

Independent, protective, and shaped by hardship, Seele tends to operate solo and prioritizes shielding others over seeking recognition for herself — a fighting style and worldview the wiki describes as directly reflecting her difficult upbringing. She's guarded with new people but fiercely loyal once trust is established.

## Faction

A member of Wildfire, the Underground Belobog resistance movement. See [`factions/belobog-factions.md`](../../factions/belobog-factions.md).
