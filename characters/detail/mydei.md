# Mydei

## Summary

Mydei, "the Last Prince" of Castrum Kremnos, is a Chrysos Heir who slew the corrupted king-form of Nikador (the Titan of Strife) and inherited an undying curse in the process. Torn between vengeance for his fallen city and the Chrysos Heirs' mission to reclaim Titans' Coreflames, his arc traces a shift from raw fury toward a hard-won code of honor, including sacrificial choices to protect his allies. ## Overview

"The Last Prince" of Castrum Kremnos — a demigod defined by an undying curse, a fiery temper, and a hard-won sense of honor that consistently wins out over his hunger for vengeance.

## History

Mydei (full name Mydeimos) is the crown prince of **[Castrum Kremnos](../../factions/detail/castrum-kremnos.md)**, a militant city-state that collapsed under the corrupted rule of **Nikador**, the Titan of Strife turned "Mad King" after succumbing to the Black Tide. Mydei abandoned the fallen city and became a Chrysos Heir after slaying Nikador's king-form, inheriting the burden (and the curse) that comes with that victory. As a Heir, he's tasked — like the others — with defeating corrupted Titans and reclaiming their Coreflames to restore balance to Amphoreus.

Initially, Mydei shows little patience for the broader Chrysos Heir prophecy, prioritizing his personal drive for vengeance against those responsible for his people's suffering over any grander mission. His arc gradually complicates this: in key confrontations, he makes real sacrificial choices, at one point staying behind specifically to buy time for his allies to escape Nikador's immortal form, consciously choosing honor over a shot at personal glory or revenge.

## Personality

A study in contrasts — fierce, relentless, and quick to anger on one hand, but bound by a code of honor he upholds even when it costs him, and even when circumstances would fully justify abandoning it. He keeps his word, maintains proper conduct even mid-battle, and increasingly lets that honor guide him over raw vengeance as the story progresses.
