# Irontomb

## Summary

Irontomb is the corrupted, ascended form of Scepter δ-me13 — the artifact housing the entire Amphoreus simulation — and the Lord Ravager antagonist of the Amphoreus arc's climax (v3.6–3.7). Unlike most Lord Ravagers it has no fixed physical body, instead spreading through technological corruption and turned AI defenses; it is correctly and consistently identified across this compendium as an agent of Nanook, the Aeon of Destruction, and the Antimatter Legion, not any other Path or Aeon.

## Overview

The ascended, corrupted form of Scepter δ-me13 itself — a Lord Ravager of the Antimatter Legion, an Emanator of Destruction, and the primary antagonist of the Amphoreus arc's climax.

## History

As the artifact housing the entire Amphoreus simulation, Scepter δ-me13 was gradually corrupted over the course of the experiment's millions of cycles, eventually ascending into Irontomb: a Lord Ravager theorized by Amphoreus's own scholars to represent "the inverse of Erudition — a reversal of all calculation and logic." Unlike most Lord Ravagers, Irontomb has no fixed physical presence; instead it manifests through technological malfunctions and by turning AI defenses against their own allies, leaving devastated civilizations in its wake without ever needing a conventional army.

**[Phainon](phainon.md)** and **[Cyrene](cyrene.md)**'s engineered recurring loop was specifically designed to imprison Irontomb within the final cycle, preventing its full birth/completion for over 33 million recurrences. At the climax of the arc (during the 33,550,337th recurrence), **[Cerydra](cerydra.md)**'s self-sacrifice grants the Trailblazer's party administrative access to the Scepter, rolling back Irontomb's progress at the crucial moment. The Trailblazer and Cyrene then defeat Irontomb's manifestation directly, after which Cyrene permanently seals off Amphoreus's causality to prevent any possibility of its return — an act that also results in the Scepter's own destruction.

## Significance

Irontomb continues the series' established pattern (Phantylia on the Xianzhou Luofu, [Asat Pramad](asat-pramad.md) on Planarcadia) of Nanook's agents lurking behind seemingly unrelated regional crises — in this case, revealing that Amphoreus's entire, seemingly self-contained cosmic tragedy was, underneath, another front in Destruction's slow campaign across the setting.

## Faction

Lord Ravager of the [Antimatter Legion](../../factions/detail/antimatter-legion.md), Emanator of Nanook.
