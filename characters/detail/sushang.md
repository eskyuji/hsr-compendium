## Overview

An earnest young swordswoman training to become a full Cloud Knight — one of the Luofu's most straightforwardly wholesome, hardworking supporting characters.

## History

Sushang trains diligently toward Cloud Knight status, driven by sincere dedication rather than family pressure or debt, in contrast to some of her more reluctant peers. She's a consistent, reliable presence in the crew's Xianzhou-set side stories, including the later Heliobi ghost-hunting content alongside Huohuo.

## Personality

Energetic, earnest, and eager to prove herself, Sushang approaches her training and duties with an enthusiasm that stands out against the Luofu's more world-weary veterans. Her sincerity makes her an easy, grounding presence for the Trailblazer's crew to work alongside.
