# Yanqing

## Summary

Yanqing is the youngest lieutenant in Cloud Knights history, a sword-obsessed prodigy of unknown lineage personally recruited and trained by General Jing Yuan of the Xianzhou Luofu. His arc centers on his devotion to swordsmanship and his ambition to become Sword Champion of the Luofu, an ambition sharpened after a formative encounter with the legendary swordmaster Jingliu. The document's core allegiance claims (Cloud Knights lieutenant, Jing Yuan's protégé) check out against available lore, but a few specific named details — the mechabeast "Windguard," the ultimate technique "Cascading Laceration," and its demonstration against Dan Heng and Blade — could not be independently verified this pass and should be checked against current in-game character story text.

## Overview

The youngest lieutenant in Cloud Knight history, and a sword-obsessed prodigy whose devotion to his craft borders on the totalizing.

## History

Of no known lineage, Yanqing was championed for military recruitment by **[Jing Yuan](jing-yuan.md)** himself, over significant public opposition given his youth and unknown background. He silenced skeptics with a spectacular battlefield debut, defeating the massive mechabeast Windguard, and has continued to rack up accolades against various threats since. Jing Yuan discovered him as a child and has personally trained him in both swordsmanship and warfare ever since, deliberately using rigorous training duels to teach him restraint alongside raw strength.

During his Companion Mission, Yanqing encounters the legendary swordmaster **Jingliu**, whose Ultimate technique directly inspires him to develop his own signature move, "Cascading Laceration" — first demonstrated against **[Dan Heng](dan-heng.md)** and **[Blade](blade.md)**.

## Personality

Single-minded about swordsmanship to a degree that's equal parts endearing and financially ruinous: "Interests are temporary, my sword is forever!" He obsessively collects antique blades from the Artisanship Commission, routinely bankrupting himself and having to beg Jing Yuan for support. His stated ambition is to become Sword Champion of the Luofu and, eventually, Sword Preeminence of the entire Xianzhou Alliance — a title left vacant for centuries since a legendary master's downfall.

## Related figures

**Jing Yuan** (mentor and commanding officer), **Jingliu** (inspirational figure and indirect teacher via observation).
