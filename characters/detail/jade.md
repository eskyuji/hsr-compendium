# Jade

## Summary

Jade is a senior manager in the IPC's Strategic Investment Department and one of the corporation's most powerful executives, the "Ten Stonehearts," known as a composed, calculating moneylender who wields debt and contracts rather than force. Her core faction identity (IPC, Ten Stonehearts, Strategic Investment Department) is well-supported by external references and unchanged from the prior draft. The specific framing of her story appearance — tied to a named "v2.4 Currency Wars" arc and a post-Penacony reconstruction loan — could not be independently verified during this pass and has been hedged accordingly; a follow-up check against the in-game story quest log is recommended.

## Overview

A senior IPC Strategic Investment Department operative and one of the Ten Stonehearts, introduced as the human face of the corporation's post-Penacony financial maneuvering.

## History

Jade appears in Penacony-era story content pressing the IPC's financial claims, in the context of debts and financial arrangements the corporation has extended to parties on Penacony (including, per some accounts, the Family). Where earlier IPC representatives like **[Topaz](topaz.md)** or **[Aventurine](aventurine.md)** engage their targets through direct negotiation or personal stakes, Jade's arc foregrounds the IPC's subtler, more systemic tool of choice: economic leverage and debt as an instrument of long-term control. (Note: the exact story quest name and patch version for this arc — previously labeled "v2.4 Currency Wars" — could not be confirmed against current sources and should be double-checked against the in-game archive.)

## Personality

Composed and calculating, Jade embodies the IPC's institutional patience — comfortable letting numbers and contracts do the work that a more openly confrontational agent might try to force. Her presence in the story underscores that the IPC's most dangerous weapon isn't force, but finance.
