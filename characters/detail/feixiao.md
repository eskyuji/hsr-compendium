# Feixiao

## Summary

Feixiao is "The Great General," the foremost of the Xianzhou Yaoqing's Seven Arbiter-Generals and commander of the elite unit Merlin's Claw, a warrior renowned across the flagship for her decisive, militant leadership. She follows the Path of the Hunt and lives under the shadow of the Moon Rage affliction, which limits her lifespan and drives her urgency to root out threats. Her v2.5 side story "Finest Duel Under the Pristine Blue," set at the Skysplitter region, briefly returns the Astral Express crew to Xianzhou territory and contrasts Yaoqing's overtly militant culture with the Luofu's diplomacy-first style under Jing Yuan. No Aeon- or faction-allegiance errors were found, but her title and rank were corrected for accuracy.

## Overview

One of the Xianzhou Yaoqing's Seven Arbiter-Generals — the majority-Foxian flagship of the [Xianzhou Alliance](../../factions/xianzhou-alliance.md) — and the central figure of the v2.5 "Finest Duel Under the Pristine Blue" side story.

## History

Known as "The Great General" and commander of the elite unit **Merlin's Claw**, Feixiao is the foremost of Yaoqing's Seven Arbiter-Generals, with a reputation for decisive, effective leadership. She follows the **Path of the Hunt** and bears the **Moon Rage affliction**, a condition that limits her lifespan and sharpens her drive to hunt down threats within the time she has. Her v2.5 story arc, set at the Skysplitter region, briefly returns the Astral Express crew to Xianzhou territory after the main run of the Penacony arc, giving a window into a different flagship's culture and command style compared to the Luofu's more diplomacy-and-commerce-oriented leadership under **[Jing Yuan](jing-yuan.md)**.

## Personality

Commanding, disciplined, and forthright, Feixiao represents Yaoqing's more overtly militant character within the wider Xianzhou Alliance, contrasting with the Luofu's more hospitable, negotiation-first culture.
