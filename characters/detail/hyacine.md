# Hyacine

## Summary

Hyacine is the Chrysos Heir who bears the Coreflame of Aquila, the Titan of the Sky and protector of Okhema, making her Amphoreus's premier healer. Warm and approachable in contrast to more burdened Heirs like Phainon or Mydei, she supports both the Chrysos Heirs and Okhema's people through the arc's conflicts. The entry's core claims about her title, Coreflame, and faction status appear consistent with established lore, though the precise circumstances of how she obtained Aquila's Coreflame could not be independently reverified this pass.

## Overview

Demigod of the Sky, after obtaining the Coreflame of Aquila — and widely regarded as Amphoreus's premier healer.

## History

Hyacine inherited her power after Aquila, the Titan of the Sky and protector of **[Okhema](../../factions/detail/okhema.md)**, passed on the Coreflame governing day-night cycles and the heavens. As a healer, she plays a crucial supportive role for the Chrysos Heirs and Okhema's people alike throughout the arc's escalating conflicts.

## Personality

Bubbly, warm, and kind, Hyacine stands out among the Amphoreus cast for her comparatively light, approachable demeanor — a contrast to the heavier, more burdened personalities of Heirs like **[Phainon](phainon.md)** or **[Mydei](mydei.md)**.

## Faction

Chrysos Heir, demigod of the Sky. See [`factions/chrysos-heirs-and-titans.md`](../../factions/chrysos-heirs-and-titans.md).
