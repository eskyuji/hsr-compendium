# Anaxa

## Summary

Anaxa is a Chrysos Heir of Amphoreus and a playable Quantum character whose in-game Path is Erudition — not "Reason," which is not an actual Path in Honkai: Star Rail. He inherited his Erudition authority through an unusual fusion with the Titan Cerces rather than defeating the Titan in combat, setting him apart from most other Heirs. He is characterized as a highly analytical scholar-strategist who aids the Trailblazer's party against the Irontomb Curse. His membership among a group termed the "Seven Sages" could not be independently verified and should be double-checked against primary sources.

## Overview

One of the Seven Sages of Amphoreus and demigod of Erudition, after fusing with the Titan Cerces.

## History

Unlike Heirs who defeated their Titan in direct confrontation, Anaxa's ascension came through fusion with Cerces, the Titan of Erudition — a distinction that sets him apart within the Chrysos Heir roster and reflects his intellectual, rather than purely martial, path to power. As one of the Seven Sages, he holds a recognized position among Amphoreus's scholarly elite.

## Personality

Exceptionally intelligent and analytically minded, Anaxa approaches Amphoreus's crises as problems to be reasoned through rather than simply fought — a disposition that makes him a valuable strategic asset to the Trailblazer's party as the [Irontomb](irontomb.md) threat escalates.

## Faction

Chrysos Heir, demigod of Erudition; member of the Seven Sages. See [`factions/chrysos-heirs-and-titans.md`](../../factions/chrysos-heirs-and-titans.md).
