# Serval

## Summary

Serval Landau is the rebellious eldest daughter of Belobog's Landau family, now running the Neverwinter Workshop and fronting her rock band, Mechanical Fever, after an earlier stint with the Silvermane Guards ended in her dismissal by Cocolia. She serves no Path or Aeon faction — she is an independent civilian figure whose defiance is aimed at Belobog's rigid, institutional culture rather than any cosmic power. Her contrast with her friend Gepard, captain of the Silvermane Guards, is a personality/temperament pairing rather than a literal familial or factional bond.

## Overview

The rebellious eldest daughter of the Landau family, a talented mechanic, and Belobog's resident rock-and-roll enthusiast — a deliberately loud, colorful counterpoint to the city's icy formality.

## History

Serval was once close with **[Cocolia](cocolia.md)** and worked for the **[Silvermane Guards](../../factions/detail/silvermane-guards.md)**, but was mysteriously fired — a dismissal she treats not as a grievance but as a liberation ("I really owe her a debt!"). She now runs the **Neverwinter Workshop**, offering repairs on everything from radiators to automatons, though she spends most of her actual time and passion modifying musical instruments for her band, **Mechanical Fever**.

## Personality

Carefree, passionate, and allergic to corporate or institutional constraint, Serval treats rock music as essential emotional release ("No matter what your mood is, you always have to vent sometimes. And rocking out"). Her workshop doubles as a performance venue and a gathering place for Belobog's youth looking for artistic expression in an otherwise rigidly controlled, frozen city.

## Family

Younger — in personality, not necessarily age — sibling contrast to **[Gepard](gepard.md)**, captain of the Silvermane Guards; despite their very different relationships to institutional duty, Gepard affectionately notes she remains "exactly the same person" she's always been, even if her popular songs occasionally distract him on duty. Note: Serval and Gepard are not blood relatives — this is a narrative/personality pairing, not a literal family tie.

## Faction

Independent; formerly Silvermane Guards-affiliated. See [`factions/belobog-factions.md`](../../factions/belobog-factions.md).
