# Yao Guang

## Summary

Yao Guang is an Arbiter-General of the Xianzhou Yuque, holding the title of Seer Strategist, introduced in v4.0 as one of Planarcadia's key new figures. Though she once perceived fortune through the "eye of the Hunt" (the Path of the Hunt), she has since forsaken that Path and embraced the Path of Elation — the same Path followed by Aha — making her less an outside foil to Planarcadia's spectacle-driven chaos and more a convert to it. Her presence on Planarcadia ties the Xianzhou Yuque, a lesser-known Xianzhou flagship, directly into the unfolding v4.x story.

## History

Yao Guang once perceived fortune and probability through what's described as "the eye of the Hunt" — a mark of her original allegiance to the Path of the Hunt. She has since undergone a radical, "mysterious, bold, and revolutionary" shift, forsaking that Path to embrace the Path of Elation, the same Path followed by Aha and expressed through Planarcadia's chaotic, spectacle-driven Phantasmoon Games. Her presence on Planarcadia ties one of the Xianzhou Alliance's other flagships (beyond the already-familiar Luofu and Yaoqing) directly into the unfolding v4.x story.

## Personality

Composed and strategically minded, Yao Guang brings a diviner's precision to Planarcadia's carnival-like atmosphere. Rather than standing opposed to Aha's embrace of chaos and spectacle, her arc is defined by having embraced that same philosophy herself, turning her foresight-driven instincts toward Elation rather than away from it.
