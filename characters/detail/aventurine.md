# Aventurine (Kakavasha)

## Summary

Aventurine (Kakavasha) is an IPC Ten Stonehearts executive whose polished corporate persona masks the trauma of surviving the destruction of his people, the Avgin of Sigonia, and a Cornerstone tied to that history. During the Penacony arc he pursues his own agenda regarding the Family's secrets while clashing with Acheron over methods, culminating in a harrowing sequence where a Harmony-enforced truth mechanism leaves him racing against a fatal countdown after being caught in a lie. The document's core claims about his IPC rank, origin, and Penacony arc role are consistent with available lore; the exact "17 hours" figure and precise mechanics of the Harmony-enforced oath are flagged for closer verification.

## Overview

An IPC "Stoneheart" executive whose smooth corporate persona conceals a survivor's desperation and a personal agenda that runs far deeper than his professional one.

## History

Born Kakavasha, Aventurine is a survivor of the **Avgin people of Sigonia**, a population devastated under circumstances the story treats as a formative trauma underlying everything he does as an adult. He rose through IPC ranks to become one of the Ten Stonehearts, carrying a **Cornerstone** tied to his people's fate — a personal stake that motivates his pursuit of influence over Penacony well beyond simple corporate interest.

During the Penacony arc (v2.0–2.2), Aventurine clashes repeatedly with **[Acheron](acheron.md)** over investigative methods while pursuing his own agenda regarding the planet's Dreamscape and the [Family](../../factions/penacony-family.md)'s secrets. A pivotal, harrowing beat in his arc has him discover he has only "17 hours to live" after lying under Harmony enforcement — a moment that crystallizes the stakes of operating inside a world where truth itself is policed by an Aeon's power.

## Personality

Charming, composed, and dangerously good at reading a room, Aventurine wears his IPC professionalism like armor over a much rawer set of motivations. He's willing to gamble everything — his position, his safety, his life — in pursuit of goals that ultimately trace back to protecting what's left of his people.

## Faction

A member of the IPC's Ten Stonehearts. See [`factions/interastral-peace-corporation.md`](../../factions/interastral-peace-corporation.md).
