# Hysilens

## Summary

Hysilens is the Chrysos Heir demigod of the Ocean, having claimed the Coreflame of Phagousa, and serves as head commander of the Northern Empire's army. The entry's key correction is tense/causality: it previously described her as currently serving "under Imperator Cerydra" without noting that Cerydra is a former Imperator who sacrifices herself at the Amphoreus arc's climax — an act carried out via Hysilens's own blade, per the Chrysos Heirs' law-rewriting death mechanic. No faction/Aeon misattribution was found; the ocean-Titan (Phagousa) and Northern Empire commander claims check out against both external sources and the compendium's own Cerydra and Northern Empire entries.

## Overview

Demigod of the Ocean and head commander of the Northern Empire's army, after defeating the Titan Phagousa.

## History

Hysilens inherited authority over storms and water rituals after defeating Phagousa, the Titan of the Ocean, and served as the **[Northern Empire](../../factions/detail/northern-empire.md)**'s top military commander under Imperator **[Cerydra](cerydra.md)**. This position placed her at the center of the Empire's harsher, more authoritarian political culture, in contrast to the more diplomatic tone of Okhema under **[Aglaea](aglaea.md)**. At the Amphoreus arc's climax, Cerydra — per the Chrysos Heirs' law-rewriting death mechanic — allows herself to be slain by Hysilens rather than fight her off, letting Hysilens claim the right to rewrite a law of nature; this act of devotion and loss is central to Hysilens's own arc and ultimately helps enable the Trailblazer's party to defeat Irontomb (see [`arcs/detail/05-amphoreus.md`](../../arcs/detail/05-amphoreus.md)).

## Faction

Chrysos Heir, demigod of the Ocean; head commander, Northern Empire, formerly under Imperator Cerydra. See [`factions/okhema-and-amphoreus-nations.md`](../../factions/okhema-and-amphoreus-nations.md) and [`factions/chrysos-heirs-and-titans.md`](../../factions/chrysos-heirs-and-titans.md).
