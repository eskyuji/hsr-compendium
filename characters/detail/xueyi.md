# Xueyi

## Summary

Xueyi is a judge of the Xianzhou Luofu's Ten-Lords Commission whose original body was destroyed by Mara corruption; she now operates from a mechanical "ingenium" puppet body built with Aurumaton technology to continue pursuing and capturing criminals. Her stoic, dutiful exterior masks real emotional depth, most visible in the rationed time she earns to spend with her sister Hanya. The document's core claims (Ten-Lords Commission judge role, ingenium puppet body, sister Hanya, weapon of chain and blade) are broadly consistent with established lore, though a few specific details noted below could not be independently re-verified this pass.

## Overview

A judge of the Xianzhou Luofu's Ten-Lords Commission, and one of the story's more quietly tragic supporting characters — a woman whose body was destroyed by Mara corruption, who now exists inside a mechanical puppet form to keep pursuing justice.

## History

Originally a living being, Xueyi's physical form was destroyed by mara corruption at some point before the current story. Rather than accept death outright, she agreed to inhabit an **ingenium** (a puppet body maintained by the Aurumatons), continuing her work as one of the [Ten-Lords Commission](../../factions/detail/ten-lords-and-realm-keeping-commissions.md)'s judges — specifically handling "detention," one of four judicial functions the Commission oversees. She pursues criminals with relentless focus, using a signature iron chain and Mara-Sunder Awl.

As compensation for her ongoing service, she's granted half a day in the living world for every criminal she captures — precious, rationed time she spends reuniting with her sister, **Hanya**. She maintains multiple spare ingenium bodies and can transfer her consciousness between them as needed, though this existence costs her ordinary human sensations: she cannot feel pain, or even sigh.

## Personality

Outwardly detached and dutiful — she describes herself as "a tool that sheds no blood and cries no tears" — but this stoicism sits over real emotional depth, particularly her bond with her sister and a persistent, unresolved bewilderment about her own fractured, half-living existence.

## Related figures

**Hanya**, her sister, whom she reunites with during her earned half-days among the living.
