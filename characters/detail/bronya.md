# Bronya

## Summary

Bronya is Cocolia's adopted daughter and the Silvermane Guards' second-in-command during the Jarilo-VI arc, caught between loyalty to her mother and growing doubts about Cocolia's Stellaron-influenced rule. After Cocolia dies at Everwinter Hill, Bronya chooses — with the Trailblazer's crew — to let Belobog believe her mother died a hero rather than expose the Stellaron manipulation, then takes up the mantle of Supreme Guardian to begin reuniting the city's surface and Underground populations. Her arc centers on choosing compassion and honesty over the rigid obedience of her upbringing.

## Overview

Cocolia's adopted daughter, torn for most of the Jarilo-VI arc between loyalty to her mother and her own conscience — and ultimately the person who inherits Belobog's leadership and its hardest choices.

## History

Raised by Supreme Guardian **[Cocolia Rand](cocolia.md)**, Bronya spends the early part of the Belobog arc enforcing her mother's increasingly oppressive policies, even as she privately senses something is wrong with Cocolia's judgment. As the truth about the Stellaron's role in the Eternal Freeze — and Cocolia's own secret reliance on its power — comes to light, Bronya has to reckon with the gap between the mother she loves and the ruler she's watched Cocolia become.

After Cocolia's death in the climactic confrontation at Everwinter Hill, Bronya chooses, alongside the Trailblazer's crew, to present her mother's death as a heroic sacrifice rather than expose the full truth of her manipulation — a decision made to protect Belobog's fragile unity rather than to cover up wrongdoing for its own sake. She then becomes the new Supreme Guardian, beginning the work of reuniting Belobog's long-divided surface and Underground populations.

## Personality

Disciplined and dutiful on the surface, Bronya carries real emotional weight beneath her composed exterior — the strain of enforcing policies she doesn't fully believe in, and later, the burden of leading a city through reconciliation rather than further division. Her arc is fundamentally about choosing compassion and honesty over the rigid obedience she was raised on.

## Faction

Formerly enforcing Silvermane Guard policy under Cocolia; becomes Belobog's Supreme Guardian by the end of the Jarilo-VI arc. See [`factions/belobog-factions.md`](../../factions/belobog-factions.md).
