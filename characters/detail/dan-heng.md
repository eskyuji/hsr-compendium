# Dan Heng

## Summary

Dan Heng is the Astral Express's reserved archivist and swordsman whose composed exterior conceals his connection to Dan Feng, a legendary exiled Xianzhou hero — a truth revealed when he transforms into Imbibitor Lunae during his confrontation with Blade in the Xianzhou Luofu arc (v1.2-1.6). That revelation entangles him with Jing Yuan's and Jingliu's shared past and with Bailu's origin as the reincarnation of a draconic abomination Dan Feng created while trying to resurrect Baiheng. In the Amphoreus arc, Dan Heng undergoes a second, irregular transformation into Permansor Terrae, claiming the Coreflame of Earth from the Titan Georios despite never having been a born Chrysos Heir, paralleling March 7th/Evernight's own irregular ascension.

## Overview

The Astral Express's reserved archivist and guard, and one of the series' clearest examples of a character whose quiet, controlled present hides an enormous, dangerous past.

## History

Dan Heng joined the [Astral Express](../../factions/astral-express.md) as an exile from the Xianzhou Luofu, taken aboard after Himeko invited him along; in the early arcs he functions largely as a careful, bookish counterweight to March 7th's exuberance, serving the crew as both fighter and record-keeper.

The Xianzhou Luofu arc (v1.2–1.6) upends this understated presentation entirely. Under the pressure of the Stellaron/Mara crisis and his confrontation with **[Blade](blade.md)**, Dan Heng transforms into **Imbibitor Lunae** — revealing that he carries the identity, or is a reincarnation, of **Dan Feng**, a legendary Xianzhou hero exiled long ago for reasons tied to his own overwhelming, destructive power. General **[Jing Yuan](jing-yuan.md)** recognizes him immediately as "an old friend," though Dan Heng insists "he's not him," suggesting a complicated relationship between his current selfhood and his predecessor's legacy and guilt.

The Amphoreus arc (v3.x) adds a further layer: Dan Heng undergoes another transformation, into **Permansor Terrae**, claiming the Coreflame of Earth from the Titan Georios despite not being a born Chrysos Heir — mirroring [March 7th](march-7th.md)/[Evernight](evernight.md)'s parallel development and reinforcing that both characters are entangled with forces well beyond the Astral Express's ordinary business.

## Personality

Reserved, disciplined, and quietly self-critical, Dan Heng carries himself with a caution that in hindsight reads as the discipline of someone actively suppressing a far more dangerous self. He's deeply loyal to the crew and slow to trust outsiders with his past, a reluctance that makes sense once his connection to Dan Feng's exile becomes clear.

## Related figures

Connected to **[Bailu](bailu.md)**, who was reincarnated from a draconic abomination Dan Feng created in a failed attempt to resurrect **Baiheng**; to **Jingliu**, a legendary swordmaster from his same era; and to **Blade**, whose confrontation with Dan Heng triggers his transformation into Imbibitor Lunae during the Luofu arc.
