# Pela

## Summary

Pela Beitel is a sharp, analytical Silvermane Guard intelligence officer who assists the Trailblazer during the Jarilo-VI arc as Cocolia's manipulation of Belobog comes to light. After the crisis on Jarilo-VI is resolved, she leaves the Silvermane Guards and becomes an information specialist traveling with the Stellaron Hunters (Kafka, Silver Wolf, and Blade), later reappearing in that capacity during the Penacony arc. She is not affiliated with any Aeon or Path in a doctrinal sense — her allegiance is professional and personal (to her intelligence work and to the Stellaron Hunter crew), not religious or Path-based.

## Overview

A sharp, no-nonsense Silvermane Guard intelligence officer who brings a cool analytical mind to Belobog's otherwise emotionally charged internal conflict.

## History

Pela serves within [Silvermane Guard](../../factions/detail/silvermane-guards.md) intelligence during the Jarilo-VI arc, providing the Trailblazer's crew with information and strategic support as the truth about **[Cocolia](cocolia.md)**'s manipulation comes to light. Her role is less about personal transformation than about being one of the competent professionals who help the crew navigate Belobog's institutional politics from the inside. After the Jarilo-VI arc concludes, Pela leaves the Silvermane Guards behind and takes up work as an information analyst traveling with the **[Stellaron Hunters](../../factions/stellaron-hunters.md)** ([Kafka](kafka.md), [Silver Wolf](silver-wolf.md), and [Blade](blade.md)), a role in which she later resurfaces during the Penacony arc.

## Personality

Composed, pragmatic, and unflappable, Pela approaches crises as problems to be solved methodically rather than emotional events to react to — a useful counterbalance to the more passionate members of the cast around her.

## Faction

Formerly an intelligence officer within the Silvermane Guards during the Jarilo-VI arc; afterward an information specialist traveling with the Stellaron Hunters.
