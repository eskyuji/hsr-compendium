# Rappa

## Summary

Rappa is a Wind, Nihility-path playable character introduced in the v2.6 "Annals of Penacony's Mappou Age" story, centered on Paperfold University College. Beyond her role in that Penacony story, she has a backstory as a survivor of unethical Genius Society experimentation at "Laboratory 17," which ties her loosely to the Galaxy Rangers as a rescued/associated figure. This entry corrects a prior error that labeled her an Erudition-path character — her actual Path is Nihility.

## Overview

A Nihility-path character (Wind element) introduced in the v2.6 "Annals of Penacony's Mappou Age" story, centered on Paperfold University College.

## History

Rappa is tied to the corrupted pocket of Penacony's Dreamscape uncovered at Paperfold University in v2.6 — a story that continues the series' ongoing pattern of revisiting Penacony's dream-based society for fresh crises even after the main Family/Sunday conflict has been resolved. Separately from this Penacony story, Rappa's backstory involves having been rescued from "Laboratory 17," a site of illicit Genius Society experimentation, which is why she is also associated with the **[Galaxy Rangers](../../factions/detail/galaxy-rangers.md)**.

## Faction

Associated with Paperfold University College, Penacony. Also loosely tied to the Galaxy Rangers as a survivor rescued from Laboratory 17's experiments, rather than being a member of either group in a formal institutional sense.
