# March 7th

## Summary

March 7th is a founding member of the Astral Express crew, discovered frozen in Six-Phased Ice with no memory of her past and named for the day of her discovery. She serves as the crew's cheerful, photography-obsessed emotional anchor through every major arc from the Herta Space Station prologue onward, even as her own origin remains an open mystery. In the Amphoreus arc, she is revealed to conceal an ancient alternate persona, Evernight, an anomalous Chrysos Heir who holds the Coreflame of Time (Titan Oronyx's domain) despite lacking the normal markers of a genuine Heir — implying her amnesia is tied to forces far older than her apparent age.

## Overview

A core member of the [Astral Express](../../factions/astral-express.md) crew, first met by the Trailblazer during the Herta Space Station prologue. Cheerful, endlessly curious, and obsessed with photography, March 7th presents as the emotional heart of the found-family crew — though her own past is one of the series' oldest running mysteries.

## History

March 7th was discovered frozen inside a block of Six-Phased Ice, with no memory of who she was or how she got there; she takes her name from the day of her discovery. Rescued and effectively adopted by the Astral Express crew, she throws herself into life aboard the train with an enthusiasm that reads, on the surface, like simple sunniness — but is also clearly bound up with the anxiety of not knowing her own history.

She's present for every major arc through v3.x: the Herta Space Station crisis, the fall of Cocolia on Jarilo-VI, the unmasking of Phantylia on the Xianzhou Luofu, the Penacony crisis, and — most significantly — the Amphoreus arc, where she discovers and confronts an alternate, more ancient persona of herself called **[Evernight](evernight.md)**, a Chrysos Heir who conceals the Coreflame of Time associated with the Titan Oronyx. Unlike the setting's other Heirs, Evernight is treated as an anomaly rather than a "native" Heir — she lacks the Electronic Signal designation that marks a genuine claimant and is described as having been ejected into Path Space, rather than having earned the Coreflame by conquering Oronyx's trial in the ordinary way. This development strongly suggests March 7th's amnesia and her true nature are connected to forces and cycles far older than her apparent age.

## Personality

March 7th is warm, exuberant, and protective of her found family, masking whatever uncertainty she carries about her own identity behind a persistently upbeat, camera-toting energy. She treats the Trailblazer as a sibling from their very first meeting and is usually the character pushing the crew toward curiosity and connection rather than caution.

## Open threads

Her true origin — what she was before the ice, and what her connection to Evernight and the Coreflame of Time actually means — remains only partially resolved as of the end of the arcs covered in this compendium.
