# Topaz (& Numby)

## Summary

Topaz is an IPC "Stonehearts" debt-collection specialist from the Strategic Investment Department, accompanied by her companion Numby. She arrives on Jarilo-VI at the close of the Belobog arc to collect the planet's historical debt to the IPC, but ultimately withdraws the claim after seeing Belobog's resilience and capable new leadership, judging that respecting the planet's autonomy serves the IPC's interests better than a costly confrontation. Her introduction establishes the IPC's pragmatic, power-respecting corporate ethos that recurs throughout the series.

## Overview

An IPC "Stoneheart" debt specialist, accompanied everywhere by her small otter-like companion Numby, whose first major appearance sets the template for how the IPC operates across the rest of the series: pragmatic, power-respecting, and willing to walk away from a bad deal.

## History

Topaz arrives on Jarilo-VI at the end of the Belobog arc (v1.1) to collect on the planet's historical debts to the IPC on behalf of the Strategic Investment Department. Rather than pressing the claim by force, she ultimately withdraws it after witnessing Belobog's own resilience and capable leadership in the wake of the Antimatter Legion/Cocolia crisis — concluding that the IPC's interests are better served by respecting Belobog's autonomy than by provoking a costly conflict.

## Personality

Business-minded but not cruel, Topaz represents the more reasonable, deal-making face of the IPC — someone who follows corporate incentives faithfully but isn't blind to when a show of force would be a losing bet. Her genuine fondness for Numby also softens her introduction, giving the IPC's brand of institutional menace a much more approachable first ambassador than the corporation's later, harder-edged representatives.

## Faction

One of the IPC's "Ten Stonehearts," under the Strategic Investment Department. See [`factions/interastral-peace-corporation.md`](../../factions/interastral-peace-corporation.md).
