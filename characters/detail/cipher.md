# Cipher

## Summary

Cipher is a Chrysos Heir of Amphoreus who inherited the Coreflame of Zagreus, the Titan of Trickery, becoming the "Demigod of Mischief" known as the flying shadow. This ascension embeds unpredictability and misdirection into Cipher's role in the story, mirroring Zagreus's original domain of ushering in unforeseen trials. Cipher's affiliations are entirely internal to Amphoreus's Chrysos Heir/Titan system and carry no connection to any Path or Aeon.

## Overview

Demigod of Mischief, known as "the flying shadow," after inheriting the Coreflame of the Titan Zagreus.

## History

Cipher became a Chrysos Heir after Zagreus, the Titan of Trickery, was defeated — inheriting a domain built around unpredictability, misdirection, and unforeseen trials. As with several of the more chaos-aligned Heirs, Cipher's presence in the story tends to complicate straightforward plans and expectations, embodying Zagreus's original role as the Titan who "ushers in unpredictable trials" for the rest of Amphoreus.
