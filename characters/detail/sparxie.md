# Sparxie

## Summary

Sparxie is a high-energy, streamer-styled persona introduced in the Planarcadia arc (v4.0) as an alternate interpretation of Sparkle, the Nihility-aligned Masked Fool first seen impersonating the singer Robin during the Penacony arc. She remains a member of the Masked Fools, with her spectacle-driven presentation fitting naturally into Planarcadia's Phantasmoon Games and Wishpower-centric culture. This entry is internally consistent with the compendium's faction and arc documentation and does not conflate her Masked Fools/Nihility narrative allegiance with the Path of Elation that defines Planarcadia's setting.

## Overview

A high-energy, streamer-styled figure introduced in v4.0 as an alternate interpretation of **Sparkle**, the Masked Fool first seen impersonating **[Robin](robin.md)** during the Penacony arc — and, fittingly, a member of the Masked Fools herself.

## History

Sparxie's introduction leans directly into Planarcadia's game-show, spectacle-driven atmosphere, presenting as a kind of in-world content creator or performer persona layered over Sparkle's established Masked Fool identity. Her arrival deepens the running thread of the Masked Fools' presence at the margins of major story arcs — first at Penacony, now at the heart of the Phantasmoon Games.

## Personality

Loud, performative, and thriving on audience attention — a natural fit for a world literally built around Wishpower, the currency of spectator attention that fuels the Phantasmoon Games.

Masked Fools. See [`factions/masked-fools.md`](../../factions/masked-fools.md) and [`factions/planarcadia-factions.md`](../../factions/planarcadia-factions.md).
