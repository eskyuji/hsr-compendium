# Lingsha

## Summary

Lingsha is a 5-star Fire, Path of Abundance healer introduced in the v2.5 Xianzhou Yaoqing side story alongside Feixiao and Moze. She is a Vidyadhara physician and the new Cauldron Master of the Xianzhou Luofu's Alchemy Commission, not merely a generic "Yaoqing-affiliated" healer as previously described. Her Path of Abundance designation reflects her in-game combat role rather than any stated allegiance to the Aeon Yaoshi.

## Overview

A healer introduced alongside Feixiao in the v2.5 Xianzhou Yaoqing side story, aligned with the Path of Abundance. She is a Vidyadhara and holds the formal title of Cauldron Master of the Xianzhou Luofu's [Alchemy Commission](../../factions/detail/alchemy-and-artisanship-commissions.md) — the same institution [Bailu](bailu.md) serves as a physician — rather than being simply a local healer attached to the Yaoqing settlement.

## History

Lingsha appears as part of the supporting cast during the Skysplitter story arc on Yaoqing, providing medical and supportive expertise within **[Feixiao](feixiao.md)**'s command structure. Her introduction rounds out the v2.5 side story's cast alongside Feixiao's martial focus and Moze's frontline aggression. As Cauldron Master, she is an intelligent, quick-witted physician known for diagnosing illness through an unusually sharp sense of smell and for using incense to calm and treat those in her care, maintaining a composed, socially adept exterior even when inwardly frustrated.
