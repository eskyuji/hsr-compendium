# Qingque

## Summary

Qingque is a Xianzhou Luofu diviner in the Divination Commission — not a Cloud Knight, as the document previously stated. Her mahjong-tile combat style mirrors her real personality: she took the Commission's entrance exam only to please her parents, expected an easy public-service post, and has remained stuck at the lowest diviner rank ever since, preferring cards and books to actual duty.

## Overview

A mahjong-obsessed, deliberately lazy diviner in the Xianzhou Luofu's Divination Commission whose chaotic, tile-based fighting style mirrors her general approach to life: unbothered, unpredictable, and secretly quite skilled when it actually matters.

## History

Qingque serves nominally as a Diviner in the [Divination Commission](../../factions/detail/divination-commission.md) on the Xianzhou Luofu, though she treats the position with far less reverence than her peers — she is as likely to be found at a mahjong table racking up debts as she is on active duty. She originally passed the Commission's entrance exam mainly to please her parents, expecting a relaxed public-service job; instead she found the work far more demanding than anticipated, and despite years of experience she has remained at the lowest rank of Diviner no matter which department she transfers to. Her family's financial troubles are a recurring background thread in her characterization, providing both comic material and a hint of real pressure behind her devil-may-care attitude.

## Personality

Laid-back to the point of parody, Qingque approaches combat the same way she approaches gambling: with apparent carelessness that conceals real cunning. Her fighting style, channeled through mahjong tiles, reflects this same chaotic-but-effective philosophy.
