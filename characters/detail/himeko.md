# Himeko

## Summary

Himeko is the Astral Express's navigator and long-standing elder-sister figure, having discovered and restored the abandoned train in the v1.0 prologue and helped anchor the crew (Dan Heng, Welt Yang, March 7th, and the Trailblazer) through every major arc since. Starting in v4.3's Planarcadia arc, Asat Pramad — a Lord Ravager of the Antimatter Legion who had posed as her father for years — exposes a series of impossibilities in her official history (her survival of the Withering Curse, an earlier Phantasmoon Games victory, and her single-handed restoration of the Express), throwing her fundamental identity into doubt. The v4.4 patch ("In Ravages Does the Whistle Sound") escalates this into a rescue-mission climax involving a force called the Voracity and a "Nova Himeko" identity, with Asat Pramad defeated but Himeko's true nature still unresolved as of this writing. This entry is internally consistent with the rest of the compendium's Planarcadia coverage ([`arcs/07-planarcadia.md`](../../arcs/07-planarcadia.md), [`arcs/detail/07-planarcadia.md`](../../arcs/detail/07-planarcadia.md), [`characters/detail/asat-pramad.md`](asat-pramad.md), [`characters/npcs-v4.md`](../npcs-v4.md)).

## Overview

Navigator of the Astral Express and, for most of the story, its de facto elder-sister figure — the one who found the train, repaired it, and set its long journey in motion. As of v4.x, she has become the center of the series' most significant ongoing mystery.

## History (as established through v3.x)

Himeko discovered the long-abandoned [Astral Express](../../factions/astral-express.md) and restored it to working order, beginning the crew's journey across the stars. She recruited or welcomed aboard [Dan Heng](dan-heng.md), [Welt Yang](welt-yang.md), and eventually [March 7th](march-7th.md) and the [Trailblazer](trailblazer.md), and has functioned since the v1.0 prologue as the group's steady, guiding presence — the one who intervenes during the Herta Space Station Antimatter Legion attack, keeps the crew grounded through the Belobog, Xianzhou Luofu, Penacony, and Amphoreus crises, and generally holds the found family together.

## The v4.x revelations

Starting in v4.3, the Planarcadia arc completely destabilizes this settled picture. **[Asat Pramad](asat-pramad.md)** — a Lord Ravager of the Antimatter Legion who had spent years posing as Himeko's father — confronts her with a series of impossibilities in her official history: how she survived something called the **Withering Curse**, how she won an earlier iteration of the Phantasmoon Games, and how she single-handedly restored the abandoned Astral Express in the first place. When his attempt to trigger the Withering Curse against her fails outright, he takes this as proof that the "Himeko" traveling with the crew isn't, in some fundamental sense, the original Himeko at all.

By v4.4 ("In Ravages Does the Whistle Sound"), this crisis comes to a head: the crew races to rescue Himeko from a confrontation staged inside a surreal painting-world as a force called "the Voracity" awakens, and a "Nova Himeko" identity/form surfaces. Asat Pramad is defeated in this patch, but the full truth about Himeko's nature — genuine survivor, something that took her place, or something else entirely — is not yet resolved as of this writing.

## Personality

Warm, capable, and quietly authoritative, Himeko has always presented as one of the most grounded and trustworthy members of the crew — which is precisely what makes the v4.x revelations so destabilizing to the story's emotional core. Her calm has, in hindsight, always had an edge of mystery to it that the earlier arcs never fully explained.
