# Tribbie

## Summary

Tribbie is a demigod Chrysos Heir who claimed her title by defeating Ianus, the Titan of Passage, and now serves as high priest of Janusopolis, Amphoreus's divination-centered city-state. She is one of the "Triplets of Fate," a designation tying her closely to Amphoreus's central themes of destiny and the cyclical nature of the world. As a Chrysos Heir, her authority derives from a defeated Titan rather than from allegiance to any Aeon, distinguishing her narrative role from any Path-based faction.

## Overview

High priest of Janusopolis and demigod of Passage — one of the "Triplets of Fate" and a key figure in Amphoreus's divinatory institutions.

## History

Tribbie became a Chrysos Heir after defeating **Janus**, the Titan of Passage, inheriting command over paths, possibilities, and the mapping of potential futures. As high priest of **[Janusopolis](../../factions/detail/janusopolis.md)**, the city built around Janus's former domain, Tribbie holds a position that blends religious and civic authority — overseeing a city whose entire culture is oriented around divination and the negotiation of fate.

## Personality

As one of the "Triplets of Fate," Tribbie's identity is tied closely to the broader theme of destiny running through the Amphoreus arc — a demigod whose domain (passage, transition, possibility) puts her in an unusually direct relationship with the story's central question of whether Amphoreus's recurring cycle can ever truly be broken.

## Faction

Chrysos Heir, high priest of Janusopolis. Like other Chrysos Heirs, Tribbie's authority comes from defeating and inheriting a Titan's domain, not from serving any Aeon — she is not a Path-aligned agent (e.g., not tied to Nanook/Destruction or any other Aeon) despite the Titans' domains echoing certain Path concepts.
