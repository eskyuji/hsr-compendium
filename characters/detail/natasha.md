# Natasha

## Summary

Natasha is a physician and the founder/leader of Wildfire, the resistance and mutual-aid network that smuggles medicine and supplies to the neglected Underground of Belobog. She becomes a key ally to the Trailblazer's crew during the Jarilo-VI arc, offering medical support and access to Wildfire's contacts, including fellow Underground resident Seele. Her characterization as a compassionate, hardened caregiver whose medical work doubles as quiet political resistance is well supported. One specific causal detail in her backstory (the "Geomarrow mining" trigger for the Underground's isolation) could not be verified against reliable sources and has been flagged rather than asserted as fact.

## Overview

The Underground's physician and the leader of **[Wildfire](../../factions/detail/wildfire.md)** — the resistance and mutual-aid movement that keeps Belobog's underclass alive in the face of the surface city's neglect.

## History

Natasha built and leads Wildfire to smuggle medicine and supplies to Underground Belobog, whose residents have been effectively cut off from surface resources and left to fend for themselves by a Overworld/Silvermane Guard administration focused on the surface. She becomes a crucial ally to the Trailblazer's crew once they flee into the Underground, providing both medical support and access to Wildfire's network of allies (including **[Seele](seele.md)** and **[Sampo](sampo.md)**).

## Personality

Practical, compassionate, and hardened by the daily reality of triage in a resource-starved community, Natasha represents the Underground's quiet backbone — someone whose care for her patients doubles as an act of ongoing political resistance against the surface's indifference.
