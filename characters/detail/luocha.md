# Luocha

## Summary

Luocha is a playable character (Path of Abundance, Imaginary type) and traveling merchant-healer who temporarily accompanies the Astral Express crew during the Xianzhou Luofu arc. The document's existing claims about his mysterious coffin, protective instincts, and unresolved identity/mission are consistent with canon and appropriately hedged rather than asserting unconfirmed lore. The main addition here is his verifiable in-game Path/Element affiliation, which the original entry omitted entirely, leaving readers without the one concrete allegiance fact that is actually confirmed (as opposed to the character's deliberately unconfirmed narrative backstory).

## Overview

A mysterious, elegant traveling merchant-healer who accompanies the Astral Express crew for parts of the Xianzhou Luofu arc — one of the series' most deliberately unresolved characters. As a playable character, his combat Path is Abundance (Imaginary type), aligning his in-battle role with the Aeon Yaoshi's domain of healing and life-preservation — though, notably, the story itself never confirms that Luocha serves Yaoshi or any Aeon directly; his personal allegiances remain part of the character's central mystery.

## History

Luocha presents himself simply as a traveling merchant who brought a coffin aboard the Luofu "as per order," though the true purpose and contents of that coffin are never fully explained within the story. He assists the crew during conflicts on the Luofu, showing consistent protective instincts toward innocent bystanders, and is understood to be carrying out some kind of secret personal will or mission — bound by oaths the story treats as central to his character without ever fully spelling them out.

## Personality

Courteous, composed, and quietly watchful, Luocha maintains an air of practiced mystery even while being genuinely, materially helpful to everyone around him. He rarely volunteers information about himself, letting his actions rather than his words define how much the crew (and the player) can trust him.

## Open questions

Luocha's true identity and origin remain one of the more openly acknowledged mysteries in the series — fan theories connecting him to interdimensional travel (echoing Welt's own crossover origin) are widespread but unconfirmed within the story as told through the arcs covered by this compendium. Whether his gameplay Path of Abundance reflects any genuine service to the Aeon Yaoshi, or is merely a mechanical/thematic association, is likewise left unresolved by the narrative itself.
