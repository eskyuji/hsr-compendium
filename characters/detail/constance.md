# Constance

## Summary

Constance is a Cremator — a member of a memory-destroying splinter faction of the Garden of Recollection — who, at Kafka's direction, burned away parts of the Trailblazer's Penacony memories to shield them from Nanook's attention. In the v3.8 Penacony-revisited coda she helps the Trailblazer reconstruct the fuller, true account of the Penacony crisis via "Elio's Script." Her cooperative role in undoing her own erasure recasts the Stellaron Hunters' earlier manipulations as more protective than they first appeared, consistent with the group's actual allegiance to the Path of Finality (not to Nanook/Destruction).

## Overview

A Cremator working in connection with the [Stellaron Hunters](../../factions/stellaron-hunters.md), whose memory-erasing powers are central to the v3.8 Penacony-revisited coda's core mystery.

## History

Constance burned away portions of the Trailblazer's memories of the original Penacony arc, at **[Kafka](kafka.md)**'s direction, specifically to keep certain events hidden from Nanook's attention — an act that, whatever its underlying motive, effectively protected the Trailblazer from further scrutiny by the Aeon of Destruction. In v3.8, she helps the Trailblazer reconstruct what actually happened during the Penacony crisis by working through "**[Elio](elio.md)**'s Script," restoring the fuller picture of events the original arc had left incomplete.

## Personality

Constance's willingness to help undo her own erasure — rather than protect the secrecy of her work — suggests a more cooperative, even remorseful relationship to her role than her introduction as a tool of the Stellaron Hunters might initially suggest.

## Faction

A Cremator — a splinter faction of the **[Garden of Recollection](../../factions/detail/garden-of-recollection.md)** (the faction devoted to Fuli, the Aeon of Remembrance) that selectively destroys memories rather than preserving them — working in cooperation with (or under the direction of) the Stellaron Hunters. Note that the Stellaron Hunters are themselves tied to the Path of Finality, not to Nanook/Destruction; Constance's erasure was done to protect the Trailblazer from Nanook, not on Nanook's behalf.
