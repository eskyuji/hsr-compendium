# Black Swan

## Summary

Black Swan is a Memokeeper of the Garden of Recollection, the faction devoted to Fuli, the Aeon of Remembrance. Introduced during Penacony's "Sound and the Fury" arc, she combines clairvoyant abilities and a genuine fascination with memory and emotion with a secret, undisclosed collaboration with Aventurine. The v3.8 Penacony-revisited coda reinforces her underlying integrity, showing she refuses to exploit the Cremators' memory-erasing powers even when given the opportunity.

## Overview

A Memokeeper of the **[Garden of Recollection](../../factions/detail/garden-of-recollection.md)** — a faction devoted to Fuli, the Aeon of Remembrance — whose clairvoyant abilities and genuine fascination with memory and emotion make her one of Penacony's most quietly powerful guides.

## History

Black Swan uses a crystal ball to glimpse potential futures, and demonstrates extensive, almost eerie knowledge of Penacony's inhabitants from the moment she's introduced. She assists the crew directly during the "Sound and the Fury" mission, helping defeat a dangerous Memory Zone creature, and subsequently guides the Astral Express through the Dreamscape's deeper, more dangerous layers — all while secretly collaborating with **[Aventurine](aventurine.md)** toward goals the story keeps deliberately undisclosed for much of the arc.

The v3.8 Penacony-revisited coda underscores her integrity: faced with the opportunity to exploit the Cremators' memory-erasing powers (as Kafka does via the Cremator **[Constance](constance.md)**, who erases and later helps reconstruct the Trailblazer's Penacony-era memories), Black Swan explicitly refuses, reinforcing that her fascination with memory and experience doesn't extend to abusing it.

## Personality

Sophisticated, enigmatic, and driven by a real hunger to "experience dazzling, cherished memories" — her curiosity about human experience reads as more genuine than predatory, even as her methods (secrecy, calculated intention, working multiple angles at once) keep her allies slightly off-balance about her true aims.

## Faction

Memokeeper, Garden of Recollection (devoted to Fuli, the Aeon of Remembrance).
