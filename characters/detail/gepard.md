# Gepard Landau

## Summary

Gepard Landau is the honorable captain of the Silvermane Guards on Jarilo-VI and the elder brother of Serval. Though he serves Supreme Guardian Cocolia loyally at first, the growing revelation of her Stellaron-driven corruption forces him to break with the institution and side with Bronya and the Trailblazer, taking a near-fatal hit shielding Bronya from Cocolia's final assault. The existing entry's framing of his arc and family ties checks out against the lore; the main gap was that it omitted his climactic injury at Everwinter Hill, which this revision adds.

## Overview

A Silvermane Guard captain whose rigid sense of honor ultimately puts him at odds with the very institution he serves — and whose personal integrity becomes one of the clearest moral compasses in the Jarilo-VI arc.

## History

Gepard is one of the first Belobog residents to welcome the Astral Express crew, embodying the [Silvermane Guards](../../factions/detail/silvermane-guards.md)' better instincts even as the institution around him grows increasingly compromised under **[Cocolia](cocolia.md)**'s secret manipulation. As evidence of Cocolia's deception mounts, Gepard is forced to choose between institutional loyalty and his own conscience — a choice he ultimately resolves by siding with the Trailblazer against the Supreme Guardian he once served without question. At the climactic confrontation at Everwinter Hill, when a Stellaron-consumed Cocolia turns her power on the group, Gepard throws himself between the attack and **[Bronya](bronya.md)**, taking the brunt of it and being left critically injured and encased in ice — a sacrifice that keeps him out of action through the rest of the immediate crisis even as Cocolia is ultimately defeated.

## Personality

Honorable to a fault, disciplined, and quietly protective, Gepard represents the Silvermane Guards' ideal self-image — the version of the institution that actually lives up to its stated principles, in contrast to how the organization functions under Cocolia's compromised leadership.

## Family

The elder brother of **[Serval](serval.md)**, the rebellious mechanic and rock musician who left the Silvermane Guards years earlier; the contrast between the two siblings' very different relationships to duty and institutional life is a recurring, affectionate thread in Belobog's supporting cast.
