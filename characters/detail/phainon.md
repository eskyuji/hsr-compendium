# Phainon (Khaslana)

## Summary

Phainon (Khaslana) is the demigod of World-bearing and a Chrysos Heir uniquely without a personal curse, who alongside his companion Cyrene used "the gaze of Fuli" to trap Amphoreus's final cycle in eternal recurrence — an act meant to prevent Irontomb, a Lord Ravager serving the Aeon of Destruction, from fully manifesting and unleashing catastrophe on the wider cosmos. Across an enormous, sacrificial number of loops, Phainon has repeatedly died and been forced to end Cyrene's life to reset the cycle. The Trailblazer's arrival breaks this pattern, and Phainon redirects them to seek a permanent way to defeat Irontomb instead of resetting again.

## Overview

The demigod of World-bearing and, by the time the Trailblazer arrives, the most burdened figure on all of Amphoreus — the only Chrysos Heir who never inherited a personal curse, yet has carried the weight of the entire recurring cycle alone.

## History

Real name Khaslana, Phainon became a Chrysos Heir without the curse that defines every other Heir's origin story. In the original iteration of Amphoreus's final cycle, he and his closest companion **[Cyrene](cyrene.md)** found a way to interrupt the simulation's deterministic march toward apocalypse: using power drawn from "the gaze of Fuli," they trapped the world in an endlessly recurring loop of that same final cycle, hoping to eventually find a way to actually win rather than simply reset to disaster each time.

The cost of this loop falls almost entirely on Phainon. Across an immense number of recurrences (commonly cited as in the tens of millions), he has repeatedly sacrificed himself in the effort to prevent the birth of **[Irontomb](irontomb.md)**, the Lord Ravager the loop's failure would unleash — and, in a particularly brutal twist, each new iteration requires him to end Cyrene's life himself in order to reset the loop and try again. Irontomb is itself an Emanator of Destruction and Lord Ravager of the Antimatter Legion, meaning the loop exists specifically to keep Nanook's Destruction from fully consuming Amphoreus and, through it, the wider cosmos. The Trailblazer's unscripted arrival finally disrupts this cycle; Phainon sends them back to the beginning of the current loop specifically to search for a way to defeat Irontomb for good, rather than simply resetting once more.

## Personality

Phainon carries himself with the exhaustion of someone who has died and killed the person he loves most an almost unfathomable number of times, all in service of a goal he can't fully explain to anyone still living a normal, linear existence. His hope, by the time the Trailblazer meets him, is worn thin but not extinguished.

## Related figures

**Cyrene** (closest companion and repeated victim/co-conspirator of the loop), **Irontomb** (the threat his sacrifice is meant to prevent), the **Trailblazer** (whose arrival finally offers a path out of the cycle).

## Faction

Chrysos Heir, demigod of World-bearing. Not an agent of Destruction himself — his and Cyrene's loop was built specifically to contain Irontomb, a servant of the Aeon of Destruction (Nanook), not to advance its cause.
