# Welt Yang

## Summary

Welt Yang is a founding passenger of the Astral Express, introduced during the Herta Space Station prologue, whose calm, elder-statesman demeanor conceals a much deeper history as a crossover figure from the broader Honkai franchise. His combat Path in Star Rail is Erudition, and his formidable, mysterious power (including black-hole/gravity-based abilities) stems from his identity as the Herrscher of the Void from Honkai Impact 3rd — not from any tie to Nanook or the Aeon-Path of Destruction, a distinction this entry corrects. Through the Jarilo-VI, Xianzhou Luofu, and Penacony arcs he serves as a stabilizing, protective mentor figure to the rest of the crew.

## Overview

A powerful, philosophical passenger of the Astral Express whose calm, elder-statesman demeanor conceals a past as a crossover figure from the wider Honkai franchise, where he is known as the Herrscher of the Void.

## History

Welt is first properly introduced during the Herta Space Station prologue, where he steps in to protect the Trailblazer amid the dangers of the derelict station after the newly-implanted Stellaron draws hostile attention. His ability to intervene with such overwhelming, gravity/black-hole-based power hints that Welt's own history involves much more than simply being "a passenger" — a thread the series treats as backstory rather than something resolved on-screen within the arcs covered by this compendium. This power and backstory derive from his identity as the Herrscher of the Void, a title and role from Honkai Impact 3rd, rather than from any allegiance to Nanook, the Aeon of Destruction — the two should not be conflated.

He remains with the crew through the Jarilo-VI, Xianzhou Luofu, and Penacony arcs, generally serving as a stabilizing, protective presence — notably staying grounded in reality during Penacony's most dangerous Dreamscape incursions specifically to ensure the crew has a safety net if things go wrong.

## Personality

Measured, wise, and world-weary in a way that reads as hard-won rather than jaded. Welt functions as an older-brother or mentor figure to the rest of the crew, someone whose calm under pressure comes from having already lived through worse.

## Note

Welt's fuller backstory (his identity as the Herrscher of the Void and his life prior to joining the Astral Express) is treated in the broader Honkai franchise more than it is re-explained within Star Rail's v1.x–v4.x main story; this compendium notes what's directly relevant to the Star Rail arcs it covers rather than importing the full crossover history. His in-game Path is Erudition — this is a gameplay classification distinct from his narrative backstory as the Herrscher of the Void, and neither should be conflated with the Aeon Nanook or the Path of Destruction.
