# Clara

## Summary

Clara is a young girl of the Jarilo-VI/Belobog arc who was raised by the ancient guardian robot Svarog after being found abandoned; her arc traces a shift from pure reliance on Svarog's cold logic to embracing her own emotional judgment, which becomes key to protecting Belobog's Underground. She befriends Hook and becomes a quiet protector of the Underground's vagrant community. This entry's claims align with established HSR lore and do not contain the Stellaron Hunters/Nanook-style Path-Aeon conflation error found elsewhere in the compendium — Clara's document does not make any faction/Aeon allegiance claims that would be subject to that error.

## Overview

A gentle, initially withdrawn young girl who pilots the towering ancient guardian robot Svarog — and whose growth from isolation to leadership is one of the Jarilo-VI arc's quieter emotional throughlines.

## History

Clara was found abandoned in a landfill southeast of Boulder Town, in an unstable mental state, before being discovered and effectively raised by **[Svarog](svarog.md)**, an ancient robot built to protect the mining district. Svarog's rigid, logic-driven worldview became the foundation of Clara's own understanding of right and wrong — she describes his calculations as "infallible" for much of her early life.

Deeply introverted and initially resistant to human connection, Clara's first real breakthrough comes through her friendship with **Hook**, whom she calls her first friend. From there she gradually draws a community of Underground vagrants around Svarog's base, becoming something like their protector and quiet leader. Over the course of the story, she comes to realize that Svarog's pure logic can't solve every problem — particularly human suffering — and that her own capacity for sincere, passionate emotion is something Svarog's calculations themselves eventually recognize as a path to reversing Belobog's doom, a moment that reframes their entire relationship from caretaker-and-ward to true partners.

## Personality

Shy, perceptive, and possessed of a tenacity that belies her age, Clara's central desire is unity: she wants "all Underworlders to become a family," a goal that mirrors the Astral Express's own found-family structure and drives much of her arc's emotional core.

## Related figures

**Svarog** (guardian and surrogate father figure), **Hook** (first friend).
