# Evanescia

## Summary

Evanescia is a "Special Observer" character introduced in Honkai: Star Rail v4.2, mechanically tied to the Phantasmoon Games' Wishpower/attention system — she builds toward a "Certified Banger" meter and triggers a "Master Fox" ability at set thresholds. Her broader narrative role in the unfolding Planarcadia arc is still being revealed. Her only documented affiliation is with the Phantasmoon Games' observer structure, not with any Aeon or Path directly, which is consistent with the rest of this compendium's treatment of the Games as an institution overseen by Aha (Elation) rather than a Path-aligned faction.
