# Aglaea

## Summary

Aglaea is the political leader of Okhema and a Chrysos Heir, having claimed the title of demigod of Romance by defeating Mnestia, the Titan of Romance. As Okhema's ruler she is a pragmatic, sometimes ruthless political operator who keeps Amphoreus's last major stable city functioning amid the arc's cascading crises — the recurring cycle, the Titans, and the eventual Irontomb threat. This is consistent with, and cross-checked against, this compendium's other Amphoreus entries (the Titans and Okhema detail docs), which independently corroborate the Mnestia/Romance and Okhema-leadership claims.

## Overview

The political leader of Okhema and demigod of Romance — a fierce, pragmatic ruler unafraid to take drastic action to protect her city.

## History

Aglaea became a Chrysos Heir after defeating **Mnestia**, the Titan of Romance, and now governs **[Okhema](../../factions/detail/okhema.md)**, the last major bastion of civilization on Amphoreus. As the city's leader, she navigates the arc's escalating political and cosmic crises — the recurring cycle, the Titans, and eventually [Irontomb](irontomb.md) — while balancing Okhema's fragile stability against outside pressure from rival nations like [Castrum Kremnos](../../factions/detail/castrum-kremnos.md) and the [Northern Empire](../../factions/detail/northern-empire.md).

## Personality

Described as "a fierce politician who doesn't shy away from taking drastic steps," Aglaea leads with a hard-nosed pragmatism that occasionally puts her at odds with more idealistic figures among the Chrysos Heirs, but which has kept Okhema functioning as Amphoreus's most stable and prosperous city despite the world's ongoing collapse.
