# Playable Characters (v2.0–2.7)

## Summary

The v2.x playable roster: the main Penacony arc's cast (Sunday, Aventurine, Acheron, Robin, Firefly, Boothill, Black Swan, Misha) plus figures introduced in the shorter post-Penacony interludes (Jade, Feixiao, Lingsha, Rappa, Fugue).

Grouped by the arc/faction where they're introduced. Descriptions focus on story role and identity, not gameplay mechanics/builds. Names link to their full profile in `detail/` where one exists.

## Introduced in the main Penacony arc (v2.0–2.2)

- **[Sunday](detail/sunday.md)** — the Oak Family's charismatic representative for the Charmony Festival; secretly conspires with the Watchmaker to seize total control of Penacony's Dreamscape. Defeated and arrested at the arc's climax; by v2.7 he has stepped back from Family leadership to live an ordinary life.
- **[Aventurine (Kakavasha)](detail/aventurine.md)** — an IPC "Stoneheart" executive and survivor of the Avgin people of Sigonia; pursues his own agenda regarding Penacony while representing IPC interests. Carries a Cornerstone tied to his people's fate.
- **[Acheron](detail/acheron.md)** — a wandering swordswoman aligned with the Path of Nihility (an Emanator of Destruction-adjacent power in some tellings), who arrives on Penacony investigating the Dreamscape's corruption while grappling with fragmented memories.
- **[Robin](detail/robin.md)** — a celebrated singer performing at the Charmony Festival; her apparent murder early in the arc sets the investigation in motion.
- **[Firefly (Sam)](detail/firefly.md)** — a genetically engineered soldier of the fallen Republic of Glamoth, operating undercover on Penacony under the Stellaron Hunter alias "Sam"; suffers from the degenerative Entropy Loss Syndrome (her full arc concludes in v2.3).
- **[Boothill](detail/boothill.md)** — a bounty hunter with a heavily augmented mechanical body, drawn into the Penacony crisis while chasing his own targets.
- **[Black Swan](detail/black-swan.md)** — a Memokeeper who guides the Trailblazer through the deeper, truer layers of Penacony's Dreamscape.
- **[Misha](detail/misha.md)** — a Reverie bellboy revealed to be a child-form dream-manifestation of the long-dormant Watchmaker, Mikhail; passes his effects to the crew before dying.

## Introduced in the post-Penacony interludes (v2.3–2.7)

- **[Jade](detail/jade.md)** — a senior IPC Strategic Investment Department operative and one of the Ten Stonehearts, who presses the IPC's financial claims on Penacony in the "Currency Wars" story (v2.4).
- **[Feixiao](detail/feixiao.md)** — Arbiter-General ("Merlin's Claw General") of the Xianzhou Yaoqing, introduced in the v2.5 Skysplitter side story.
- **[Lingsha](detail/lingsha.md)** — a healer introduced alongside Feixiao in the v2.5 Yaoqing side story.
- **Moze** — a Yaoqing-affiliated Hunt-path fighter introduced in the v2.5 side story.
- **[Rappa](detail/rappa.md)** — an Erudition-path character introduced in the v2.6 Paperfold University story.
- **[Fugue](detail/fugue.md)** — the survivor identity taken up by Tingyun after Ruan Mei saves her life following her near-fatal clash with the Ravager Phantylia on the Xianzhou Luofu; introduced in v2.7 as the crew prepares to depart Penacony.
