# Major NPCs and Antagonists (v2.0–2.7)

## Summary

The v2.x antagonist/supporting-NPC roster: Gopher Wood and the original Watchmaker Mikhail (Penacony's founding), Elio's expanded role as the Stellaron Hunters' predictive strategist, and the Aeons Xipe, Ena, and Akivili introduced in this era.

Names link to their full profile in `detail/` where one exists; Aeons link to the consolidated `detail/aeons-reference.md`.

## Penacony arc

- **[Gopher Wood](detail/gopher-wood.md)** — the current Watchmaker, secretly conspiring with Sunday to remake the Dreamscape; takes his own life once the plot unravels rather than face the consequences.
- **Mikhail ("the first Watchmaker")** — an early Trailblazer companion (alongside Tiernan and Razalina) who helped liberate Penacony's original prisoners, later partnering with the Family to build the modern Dreamscape; long dormant by the time of the story, manifesting only through the dream-child [Misha](detail/misha.md).
- **Tiernan** and **Razalina** — the other two early Trailblazers who fought alongside Mikhail during Penacony's original uprising against IPC control; honored posthumously by the crew.
- **Lady Bonajade** — an IPC-affiliated colleague of Aventurine's, commissioned by Robin, who is implicated in a late attempt to help Sunday evade full accountability.
- **[Elio](detail/elio.md)** — "Destiny's Slave," the founder and leader of the Stellaron Hunters, who can foresee future possibilities and directs the group's operations via predictive "scripts." His expanded role in the Penacony arc reveals that he foresaw the Trailblazer's eventual confrontation with Nanook, the Aeon of Destruction, and has been positioning allies accordingly — though the Trailblazer's existence has already begun disrupting his plans.

## Aeons introduced in v2.x

See [`detail/aeons-reference.md`](detail/aeons-reference.md) for full entries.

- **Xipe** — the Aeon of Harmony; bound Penacony's original prison-camp survivors into the Family.
- **Ena** — the enigmatic Aeon of Order, invoked as the philosophical justification (and, arguably, distortion) behind Sunday's scheme to remake the Dreamscape.
- **Akivili** — identified in v2.7 as the Aeon of the Trailblaze Path, described as unable to reach the realm of Amphoreus — the destination the Astral Express sets out for at the end of v2.7.
