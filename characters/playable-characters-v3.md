# Playable Characters (v3.0–3.8)

## Summary

The v3.x playable roster: the ten Chrysos Heirs who each inherited a Titan's Coreflame (Phainon, Castorice, Mydei, Aglaea, Tribbie, Cipher, Hyacine, Anaxa, Hysilens, Cerydra), plus the Astral Express crew members transformed by Amphoreus (Evernight, Permansor Terrae) and Phainon's companion Cyrene.

Grouped by role/arc context. Descriptions focus on story identity, not gameplay mechanics/builds. Names link to their full profile in `detail/` where one exists.

## The Chrysos Heirs (introduced across v3.0–3.7)

- **[Phainon (Khaslana)](detail/phainon.md)** — demigod of World-bearing; the only Heir without an inherited curse, having shouldered Amphoreus's recurring cycle alone across millions of repetitions; central to the arc's climax.
- **[Castorice](detail/castorice.md)** — daughter of the river Styx; demigod of Death; extremely kind-hearted despite her lethal powers.
- **[Mydei](detail/mydei.md)** — "the Last Prince" of Castrum Kremnos; demigod after slaying its former king; strong-willed and fair.
- **[Aglaea](detail/aglaea.md)** — political leader of Okhema; demigod of Romance; a fierce, pragmatic politician.
- **[Tribbie](detail/tribbie.md)** — high priest of Janusopolis and one of the Triplets of Fate; demigod of Passage.
- **[Cipher](detail/cipher.md)** — demigod of Mischief, "the flying shadow," after inheriting Zagreus's Coreflame.
- **[Hyacine](detail/hyacine.md)** — demigod of the Sky; bubbly and kind; widely regarded as Amphoreus's best healer.
- **[Anaxa](detail/anaxa.md)** — one of the Seven Sages; demigod of Reason; exceptionally intelligent.
- **[Hysilens](detail/hysilens.md)** — demigod of the Ocean; head commander of the Northern Empire's army.
- **[Cerydra](detail/cerydra.md)** — former Imperator of the Northern Empire; demigod/wielder of the Coreflame of Law; sacrifices herself at the arc's climax.

## Returning crew members transformed by Amphoreus

- **[Evernight](detail/evernight.md)** — an alternate, more ancient persona of March 7th, uncovered during the Amphoreus arc; claims the Coreflame of Time despite not being a born Chrysos Heir.
- **[Dan Heng, Permansor Terrae](detail/dan-heng.md)** — a further transformation of Dan Heng beyond his Imbibitor Lunae identity (see v1.x compendium); claims the Coreflame of Earth.

## Cyrene

- **[Cyrene](detail/cyrene.md)** — Phainon's closest companion and fellow architect of the recurring-cycle loop; ultimately helps the Trailblazer defeat Irontomb and permanently seals off Amphoreus's causality to prevent its return.
