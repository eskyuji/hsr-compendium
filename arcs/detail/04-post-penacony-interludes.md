# Post-Penacony Interludes — Detailed Plot, Timeline & Revelations

## Summary

This entry tracks the shorter side chapters between "Farewell, Penacony" and "A New Venture on the Eighth Dawn" (v2.3–2.7): Jade's IPC debt-collection arc, a return visit to the Xianzhou Luofu for the Wardance (introducing visiting Yaoqing/Zhuming figures Feixiao, Lingsha, and Moze), a fresh Dreamscape corruption at Penacony's Paperfold University (introducing Rappa), and the farewell chapter that sends the Express toward Amphoreus. One item has been corrected for chronology: Firefly's on-screen "fireworks" moment with the Trailblazer occurs at the close of "Farewell, Penacony" itself, but her survival is not actually confirmed, and the Path of Finality/Elio's Script material resolving her fate, is depicted much later (v3.8), not within this v2.3–2.7 window — the timeline below has been adjusted accordingly. A location error has also been fixed: the Wardance chapter takes place on the Xianzhou Luofu (Skysplitter is a Luofu locale), not aboard the Yaoqing flagship, which Feixiao merely visits from.

Covers v2.3–2.7, connecting the "Farewell, Penacony" and "A New Venture on the Eighth Dawn" Trailblaze Mission chapters with the shorter side stories between them. See [`arcs/04-post-penacony-interludes.md`](../04-post-penacony-interludes.md) for the standard summary; this document adds a fuller timeline and explicit revelations list.

## Timeline of events

### v2.3 — "Farewell, Penacony" finale and Firefly's fate (open-ended)

1. In the climactic chapters of "Farewell, Penacony," **[Firefly](../../characters/detail/firefly.md)**'s **Entropy Loss Syndrome** and the merged Dreamscape/reality crisis bring her story to a head; she dies within the dream more than once and ultimately shares a fireworks moment with the Trailblazer as Sunday is defeated — but her fate afterward is left ambiguous, not resolved, at the close of v2.3.
2. This ambiguity is *not* settled within the v2.3–2.7 window: the Trailblazer's use of the **Path of Finality** to alter the past and grant Firefly's fireworks wish, Silver Wolf's confirmation of Firefly's survival, and the fuller reveal of **Elio's Script** predicting her fate all belong to a much later chapter ("That Which Shall Be," v3.8) and are noted here only for continuity — they are out of scope for this arc's proper timeline.
3. Expanded material on **Elio's Script** more broadly reveals the founder of the Stellaron Hunters had predicted several individual character fates with unsettling precision, reinforcing how much of the Penacony crisis was quietly orchestrated from behind the scenes.

### v2.4 — "Currency Wars" (Jade and the IPC)

4. **[Jade](../../characters/detail/jade.md)**, of the IPC's Strategic Investment Department, arrives on Penacony to press the corporation's financial claims following the reconstruction loan extended at the end of the main arc.
5. The story explores the IPC's use of economic coercion and debt as a subtler, longer-term instrument of control than the more overt tactics seen elsewhere in the series.

### v2.4–2.5 — "Finest Duel Under the Pristine Blue" (Xianzhou Luofu Wardance)

6. The Astral Express returns to the **Xianzhou Luofu** — Jing Yuan's flagship, previously the setting of the main Xianzhou arc — this time for the Luofu's ceremonial **Wardance** tournament, including the **Skysplitter** region and the Shackling Prison.
7. Introduction of **[Feixiao](../../characters/detail/feixiao.md)** (Arbiter-General of the **Xianzhou Yaoqing**, visiting the Luofu for the Wardance), **[Lingsha](../../characters/detail/lingsha.md)**, and **Moze**, giving a window into a different, more overtly militant flagship culture than the Luofu's diplomacy-first approach.

### v2.6 — "Banana Outrage: Battles Without Ninja and Humanity" (Paperfold University)

8. The crew returns to Penacony to address a fresh, localized corruption within the Dreamscape centered on **Paperfold University College**.
9. Introduction of **[Rappa](../../characters/detail/rappa.md)**, tied to this new pocket of crisis — underscoring that Penacony's underlying Dreamscape instability persists even after the Family/Sunday conflict's resolution.

### v2.7 — "A New Venture on the Eighth Dawn" (Departure for Amphoreus)

10. A farewell chapter: the crew reunites with friends made across the whole Penacony era for one last set of shared memories.
11. **[Sunday](../../characters/detail/sunday.md)**, having stepped back from leading the Oak Family to live an ordinary life, gives a final performance before departing.
12. **[Fugue](../../characters/detail/fugue.md)** (formerly Tingyun; during a catastrophic Xianzhou incident orchestrated by the Lord Ravager **Phantylia**, her original identity — face, name, and former life — was stolen and she was left near death, only later reclaiming a life of her own as "Fugue." The precise in-game mechanism of her survival and return, and any role other characters played in it, is not fully spelled out and should not be over-specified) is introduced as the crew prepares to leave.
13. **Black Swan's** earlier suggestion that the crew investigate **Amphoreus** — a realm even the Aeon of the Trailblaze Path, **Akivili**, has never set foot in — comes to a head; navigator Himeko gathers intelligence and sets the Express's course for its next destination.

## Major events

- Firefly's fate is left open at the end of "Farewell, Penacony" (v2.3); full resolution and survival confirmation occur later, outside this arc's window (v3.8).
- The IPC's continued economic entrenchment in Penacony via Jade's "Currency Wars."
- A rare mid-arc return to Xianzhou territory (the Luofu, not the Yaoqing) rather than a wholly new setting, with visiting Yaoqing figures introduced.
- Identification of Akivili as the Aeon of the Trailblaze Path, and confirmation that Amphoreus is uniquely isolated even from that Aeon's reach.

## Key revelations

- Elio's predictive "scripts" extend to individual character fates with striking precision, reinforcing how much of the Penacony crisis was quietly orchestrated from behind the scenes; the fullest confirmation of this regarding Firefly specifically comes later than this arc (v3.8).
- Penacony's Dreamscape instability is an ongoing condition, not a one-time crisis resolved by Sunday's arrest — new corrupted pockets (Paperfold University) continue to surface.
- Akivili, the Trailblazer's own patron Aeon, cannot reach Amphoreus — making it a genuinely unprecedented destination for the Astral Express.

## Open threads carried forward

- Firefly's ultimate fate, left unresolved at the close of "Farewell, Penacony" (later addressed well beyond this arc, in v3.8).
- The full nature of Amphoreus and why it's beyond even Akivili's reach (addressed in [`arcs/05-amphoreus.md`](../05-amphoreus.md)).
- Fugue's ongoing arc as a survivor rebuilding identity after the Phantylia incident (the exact mechanism of her survival/return, and any other characters' role in it, remains under-documented in available sources and is flagged here rather than asserted).
