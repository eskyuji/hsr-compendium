# Jarilo-VI / Belobog — Detailed Plot, Timeline & Revelations

## Summary

This entry provides a beat-by-beat timeline of the Jarilo-VI/Belobog arc (TB missions "In the Withering Wintry Night" and "In the Sweltering Morning Sun"), covering the crew's discovery that Supreme Guardian Cocolia had suppressed and later exploited a Stellaron, their alliance with the Underground's Wildfire network, and the climactic battle at Everwinter Hill where Qlipoth (Aeon of Preservation) empowers the Trailblazer to defeat Cocolia. It closes with Bronya's ascension to Supreme Guardian, her decision to protect Belobog's unity with a comforting myth about her mother's death, and Topaz's first IPC debt-collection visit. Fact-checking confirmed the timeline, character allegiances, and the Qlipoth/Preservation blessing are accurate, with one correction: the weapon the Trailblazer draws during Qlipoth's blessing is named in mission text as **Alisa Rand's Lance**, not a generic "flaming lance" (that description is not the in-story naming). No faction/Aeon misattribution errors were found in this document.

Official Trailblaze Mission chapters: **"In the Withering Wintry Night"** and **"In the Sweltering Morning Sun"** (v1.0–1.1). See [`arcs/01-jarilo-vi-belobog.md`](../01-jarilo-vi-belobog.md) for the standard summary; this document adds a beat-by-beat timeline and an explicit list of revelations and turning points.

## Timeline of events

### Part 1: "In the Withering Wintry Night"

1. **Arrival.** The Astral Express reaches Jarilo-VI, an ice-locked world gripped by the long-running "Eternal Freeze." The crew makes contact with Belobog, the last major settlement, and its ruler, Supreme Guardian **[Cocolia Rand](../../characters/detail/cocolia.md)**.
2. **Initial welcome.** The crew is introduced to the [Silvermane Guards](../../factions/detail/silvermane-guards.md), including the honorable captain **[Gepard Landau](../../characters/detail/gepard.md)**, and begins investigating the source of the planet's endless winter.
3. **The frame-up.** As the crew gets closer to evidence that a Stellaron — not natural causes — is behind the Eternal Freeze, Cocolia moves against them, having them framed for a crime they didn't commit.
4. **Flight into the Fragmentum and the Underground.** Pursued by the Silvermane Guards, the crew escapes into the hazardous Fragmentum and from there into Underground Belobog, discovering a population of underclass citizens left to fend for themselves after Geomarrow mining ceased.
5. **New allies.** The crew connects with **[Wildfire](../../factions/detail/wildfire.md)**, the Underground's resistance/mutual-aid network led by **[Natasha](../../characters/detail/natasha.md)**, and gains new allies: **[Seele](../../characters/detail/seele.md)**, **[Sampo](../../characters/detail/sampo.md)**, and eventually **[Clara](../../characters/detail/clara.md)** and her guardian robot **[Svarog](../../characters/detail/svarog.md)**.
6. **Earning Svarog's trust.** The party works to convince the initially hostile, purely logic-driven Svarog that unpredictable human variables — emotion, choice — have real value, eventually winning him as an ally.
7. **The truth about the Eternal Freeze.** Evidence surfaces that an "old researcher" long ago proved the Eternal Freeze was caused by a Stellaron, and that the Guardian at the time suppressed the report to avoid social upheaval — a suppression Cocolia has continued and, worse, personally exploited.

### Part 2: "In the Sweltering Morning Sun"

8. **Return to the surface.** Armed with the truth, the crew returns to confront Cocolia directly, joined by **[Bronya](../../characters/detail/bronya.md)**, Gepard, and other allies who've now turned against the Supreme Guardian's regime.
9. **First confrontation at Everwinter Hill.** Cocolia reveals she has been secretly wielding the Stellaron's power directly; her assault nearly overwhelms the Trailblazer.
10. **Qlipoth's intervention.** The Trailblazer receives a vision and blessing from **Qlipoth**, the Aeon of Preservation, drawing a weapon named **Alisa Rand's Lance** and gaining the Path of Preservation along with renewed strength.
11. **Cocolia's defeat.** With this power and the support of allies, the Trailblazer defeats Cocolia; the Stellaron's hold over her breaks, and she dies in the confrontation.
12. **The noble lie.** Rather than expose Cocolia's manipulation and years of suppressed truth publicly, Bronya and the crew choose to let her be remembered as a hero who sacrificed herself for Belobog — prioritizing the city's fragile unity over full transparency.
13. **Bronya becomes Supreme Guardian**, beginning the work of reuniting Belobog's surface and Underground populations.
14. **[Topaz](../../characters/detail/topaz.md) arrives** on behalf of the IPC to collect on Belobog's historical debts, but withdraws the claim after witnessing the city's own technological strength (Svarog), setting a template for how the IPC will operate throughout the rest of the series.
15. **Departure.** The Astral Express leaves Jarilo-VI for the Xianzhou Luofu, pulled there by Kafka's manipulation.

## Major events

- Belobog's surface/Underground divide is exposed and, by the arc's end, actively being repaired.
- The death of a major named antagonist (Cocolia) resolved through both combat and a difficult ethical compromise (the "noble lie").
- First direct blessing from an Aeon (Qlipoth) granted to the Trailblazer.
- First direct IPC contact and negotiation.

## Key revelations

- The Eternal Freeze was never a natural phenomenon — it was caused by a Stellaron, deliberately suppressed from public knowledge for generations.
- Cocolia had been secretly relying on the Stellaron's power to maintain her authority, corrupting both her judgment and Belobog's governance over time.
- Bronya's willingness to preserve a comforting myth about her mother's death, rather than the full truth, previews a recurring series theme: that "the right choice" is not always the fully honest one.
