# Arc: Herta Space Station (v1.0 Prologue)

*For a detailed, beat-by-beat timeline, major events, and key revelations, see [`arcs/detail/00-herta-space-station.md`](detail/00-herta-space-station.md).*

## Summary

The game's tutorial arc. It introduces the Trailblazer, seeds them with a Stellaron, and sets the entire series in motion. It takes place aboard the **Herta Space Station**, a research facility built around a Space Anchor (a stable point used to study the surrounding Herta Nebula's Stellaron-related phenomena). Kafka and Silver Wolf implant a Stellaron in the Trailblazer during an Antimatter Legion attack, drawing a brief, ominous gaze from Nanook (Aeon of Destruction); Welt Yang intervenes to seal the Stellaron and save the Trailblazer, who then departs the station to join the Astral Express crew.

## Plot

The Stellaron Hunters **[Kafka](../characters/detail/kafka.md)** and **[Silver Wolf](../characters/detail/silver-wolf.md)** infiltrate the station during a chaotic **Antimatter Legion** attack. Kafka implants a Stellaron into the body of the [Trailblazer](../characters/detail/trailblazer.md) (the player character), who is caught in the crossfire. This act nearly kills the Trailblazer; they are saved when they instinctively shield fellow station resident **[March 7th](../characters/detail/march-7th.md)** from a lethal energy blast.

The blast — and the Stellaron now lodged in the Trailblazer's body — draws the attention of **Nanook**, the Aeon of Destruction, in a brief and terrifying vision. The Trailblazer is rescued from this confrontation by **[Welt Yang](../characters/detail/welt-yang.md)**, a mysterious passenger of the **[Astral Express](../factions/astral-express.md)** who possesses formidable combat abilities (later revealed to be tied to his own history as the former Herrscher of Reason from *Honkai Impact 3rd*, and to his mastery of the Path of Nihility).

Alongside March 7th and **[Dan Heng](../characters/detail/dan-heng.md)** (an Astral Express crew member acting as an archivist/guard), the Trailblazer helps repel the Antimatter Legion assault, aided by **[Himeko](../characters/detail/himeko.md)**, the Express's navigator, and station staff including Lead Researcher **Asta** and station director **[Herta](../characters/detail/herta.md)** (or her puppet/avatar — Herta is rarely seen in person and often "attends" via a doll-like proxy).

With the Stellaron now bonded to them and the station in crisis, the Trailblazer is given a choice: remain safely on the station, or board the Astral Express and go looking for answers about the Stellaron, the Aeon of the Trailblaze Path, and what has happened to them. They choose to leave, and the story proper begins.

## Key outcomes

- The Trailblazer is permanently bonded to a Stellaron, which grants unusual resistance to certain harms but also makes them a target/point of interest for both the Stellaron Hunters and various Aeons throughout the story.
- The Trailblazer joins the Astral Express crew alongside March 7th, Dan Heng, Himeko, and Welt Yang, forming the core cast for the rest of v1.x.
- Kafka's parting words to the Trailblazer ("there will come a time when you have to make an important choice... make the right choice") foreshadow recurring themes about fate, choice, and the Trailblaze Path.

## Notes

This arc functions primarily as a tutorial and character-introduction sequence; the deeper mystery of the Stellaron and the Trailblazer's origin is left unresolved and threaded through the rest of v1.x (and beyond).
