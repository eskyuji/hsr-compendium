# Arc: Jarilo-VI / Belobog (v1.0–1.1)

*For a detailed, beat-by-beat timeline, major events, and key revelations, see [`arcs/detail/01-jarilo-vi-belobog.md`](detail/01-jarilo-vi-belobog.md).*

## Summary

This entry covers the Astral Express's first major stop, Jarilo-VI, where the crew uncovers that Supreme Guardian Cocolia Rand has been hiding — and secretly drawing power from — a Stellaron responsible for the planet's centuries-long Eternal Freeze. Allying with the Underground resistance (Wildfire, led by Natasha, alongside Seele, Clara, and the ancient guardian mech Svarog), the Trailblazer confronts Cocolia at Everwinter Hill, aided by a blessing from Qlipoth, the Aeon of Preservation, which breaks the Stellaron's hold on her. Cocolia dies in the battle; her adopted daughter Bronya succeeds her as Supreme Guardian, works to reunite the surface and Underground populations, and the arc closes as IPC envoy Topaz stands down after witnessing Belobog's own technological strength, with the Express departing for the Xianzhou Luofu.

## Setting

- **Belobog (Surface City)** — built by "the Architects" after the Antimatter Legion devastated the planet roughly 700 years prior; ruled by a **Supreme Guardian**.
- **Underground Belobog** — a sealed-off district beneath the surface, home to former miners and their descendants, cut off from surface resources after Geomarrow mining operations ceased.
- **The Fragmentum** — a hazardous zone outside Belobog's defenses, overrun by the Antimatter Legion.
- **Everwinter Hill** and **Qlipoth Fort** — the Supreme Guardian's seat of power and the arc's climactic battleground.

## Plot

The Astral Express arrives investigating the source of Jarilo-VI's long winter. They're initially welcomed by **Supreme Guardian [Cocolia Rand](../characters/detail/cocolia.md)** and her [Silvermane Guards](../factions/detail/silvermane-guards.md), including the honorable knight **[Gepard Landau](../characters/detail/gepard.md)**. However, the crew soon uncovers that Cocolia has been suppressing evidence that a **Stellaron** — not natural causes — is responsible for the Eternal Freeze, and that she has secretly been relying on its power.

After being framed for a crime they didn't commit, the Trailblazer and allies flee into the Underground, where they discover a community of underground-born citizens surviving in poverty and neglect, protected by the resistance group **[Wildfire](../factions/detail/wildfire.md)**, led by the physician **[Natasha](../characters/detail/natasha.md)**. Here they gain new allies, including **[Seele](../characters/detail/seele.md)** (an Underground fighter with a dangerous split-personality "Quantum" ability) and **[Clara](../characters/detail/clara.md)** (a young girl who pilots the enormous mech **[Svarog](../characters/detail/svarog.md)**, an ancient guardian robot originally built to protect the mining district).

The party earns Svarog's trust, uncovers the truth of the Stellaron's role in the Eternal Freeze, and returns to the surface to confront Cocolia, whose adopted daughter **[Bronya](../characters/detail/bronya.md)** has been torn between loyalty to her mother and her own conscience.

At Everwinter Hill, the Trailblazer is initially overwhelmed by Cocolia's Stellaron-empowered assault. They are aided by a vision/blessing from **Qlipoth**, the Aeon of Preservation, gaining a new weapon (a flaming lance) and the strength to turn the tide. Cocolia is defeated, and the Stellaron's influence over her is broken.

## Resolution

- Cocolia dies in the confrontation; rather than expose her manipulation and corruption publicly, Bronya and the crew choose to let her be remembered as a hero who sacrificed herself for Belobog, to protect the city's morale and unity.
- **Bronya** succeeds Cocolia as the new Supreme Guardian, beginning reforms to reunite the surface and Underground populations.
- **[Topaz](../characters/detail/topaz.md)**, a representative of the Interastral Peace Corporation (IPC), arrives to collect on Belobog's historical debts, but ultimately backs off after being shown the city's own indigenous technological strength (Svarog).
- The Astral Express departs Jarilo-VI for the Xianzhou Luofu, setting up the next arc.
