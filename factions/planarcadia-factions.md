# Factions of Planarcadia (Index)

## Summary

Planarcadia is the not-yet-released sixth world of Honkai: Star Rail (after Amphoreus), tied to Aha, Aeon of Elation — this general framing is corroborated by the fandom wiki's "upcoming world" overview, which describes Aha's reward as inviting pursuers of Elation to "join their game and become an Aeon for a minute." Beyond that broad premise, however, most of the specific proper nouns and plot beats below — the Phantasmoon Games (by that name), the v4.2 prize escalation to *permanent* Aeon-hood, the Research Society of Happy Smiles and its leader Fulwish, and the Antimatter Legion's Lord Ravager Asat Pramad and his alleged years-long impersonation of Himeko's father — could **not** be corroborated against any available source, including a direct check of the wiki's dedicated Aha page, which does not mention any of them. Because Planarcadia has not launched as of this writing, treat everything below beyond the "sixth world / tied to Aha / Elation-themed game with an Aeon-hood reward" premise as **unconfirmed pre-release/speculative material**, not verified released content, and re-check it against official patch notes once the arc ships.

Planarcadia's central institution is a game, not a government — but that game has real political and cosmic stakes. Full write-ups live in `factions/detail/`.

- **Phantasmoon Games** *(unconfirmed pre-release detail — name and prize structure not found in any corroborating source)* — a recurring celestial competition said to be overseen by Aha, Aeon of Elation; per speculative material, its v4.2 prize is escalated to permanent Aeon-hood, which would drive the arc's central tension if accurate. → [`factions/detail/phantasmoon-games.md`](detail/phantasmoon-games.md)
- **Research Society of Happy Smiles** *(unconfirmed pre-release detail — no corroborating source found for this name or "Fulwish")* — said to be led by Fulwish; framed in speculative material as the arc's voice of institutional skepticism toward the Games' escalating stakes. → [`factions/detail/research-society-happy-smiles.md`](detail/research-society-happy-smiles.md)
