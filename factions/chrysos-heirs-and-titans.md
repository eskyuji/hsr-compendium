# Chrysos Heirs & the Twelve Titans (Index)

Amphoreus's power structure runs on a single mechanic: twelve Titans, each paired with a Coreflame, and twelve (or more, counting later irregular cases) mortal Chrysos Heirs who inherit that power by defeating, fusing with, or otherwise claiming their Titan's Coreflame. Full write-ups live in `factions/detail/`.

- **Chrysos Heirs** — the mortals who became demigods: [Aglaea](../characters/detail/aglaea.md), [Mydei](../characters/detail/mydei.md), [Tribbie](../characters/detail/tribbie.md), [Castorice](../characters/detail/castorice.md), [Phainon](../characters/detail/phainon.md), [Cipher](../characters/detail/cipher.md), [Hyacine](../characters/detail/hyacine.md), [Anaxa](../characters/detail/anaxa.md), [Hysilens](../characters/detail/hysilens.md), [Cerydra](../characters/detail/cerydra.md), plus the irregular cases [Evernight](../characters/detail/evernight.md) (March 7th) and [Dan Heng](../characters/detail/dan-heng.md), Permansor Terrae. → [`factions/detail/chrysos-heirs.md`](detail/chrysos-heirs.md)
- **The Twelve Titans** — Janus, Talanton, Oronyx (Fate); Kephale, Georios, Phagousa, Aquila (Creation); Cerces, Mnestia (Reason & Romance); Nikador, Thanatos, Zagreus (Calamity). → [`factions/detail/the-twelve-titans.md`](detail/the-twelve-titans.md)
