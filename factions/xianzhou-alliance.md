# The Xianzhou Alliance (Overview & Index)

## Summary

The Xianzhou Alliance is an immortal, space-faring civilization under the Path of the Hunt, founded after the Aeon Lan led survivors of a corrupted Ambrosial Arbor on a mission to eradicate Abominations of Abundance. It is organized as a loose federation of six flagships (Luofu, Yaoqing, Zhuming, Fanghu, Xuling, Yuque), each with its own dominant population, specialization, and Arbiter-General, rather than as a single unified actor. Core review found the document's Aeon allegiance and character/title attributions (Lan/Hunt, Jing Yuan on the Luofu, Feixiao on Yaoqing, Fu Xuan and Yukong's commission roles) consistent with established lore, so no factual corrections were required beyond adding this summary.

## Overview

An ancient, immortal, space-faring civilization following Lan, the Aeon of the Hunt (see [`characters/detail/aeons-reference.md`](../characters/detail/aeons-reference.md)). According to Xianzhou lore, the Alliance was established after Lan destroyed a corrupted Ambrosial Arbor and set the Xianzhou people on a mission to "patrol the world and eradicate all Abominations of Abundance" — a founding trauma echoed directly in the v1.2–1.6 Luofu arc, when a Stellaron is deliberately smuggled into a still-sacred Ambrosial Arbor.

## The Six Flagships

- **Luofu** — diplomacy, commerce, and hospitality; the setting of the v1.2–1.6 arc.
- **Yaoqing** — military strength; majority-Foxian population; setting of the v2.5 side story.
- **Zhuming** — craftsmanship and alchemy technology.
- **Fanghu** — an autonomous Vidyadhara dominion.
- **Xuling** — staffed entirely by Aurumatons.
- **Yuque** — the birthplace and historical home of the Divination Commission.

Each flagship has developed a distinct culture reflecting its dominant population and specialization, meaning "the Xianzhou Alliance" is rarely a single unified actor — different flagships and their Arbiter-Generals can pursue quite different priorities.

## Constituent peoples

- **Xianzhou natives (Homo celestinae)** — the original founders, effectively immortal. Their near-immortality creates a specific vulnerability: Mara corruption feeds on accumulated negative memory, so the Alliance's oldest and most experienced citizens are also its most susceptible targets — a fact exploited directly during the Luofu arc.
- **Foxians** — fox-humanoid people, rescued from historical enslavement, dominant on Yaoqing.
- **Vidyadhara** — dragon-humanoid people saved from extinction, self-governing on Fanghu.
- Smaller populations of Heliobi, Disatta, Aurumatons, and Ingenia also live within the Alliance.

## Government structure and institutions

- **Arbiter-Generals** — military commanders of each flagship, under an overall Marshal. On the Luofu: **[Jing Yuan](../characters/detail/jing-yuan.md)**. On Yaoqing: **[Feixiao](../characters/detail/feixiao.md)**.
- **Six Charioteers** — heads of the major administrative commissions.
