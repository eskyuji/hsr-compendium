# Wildfire (Detail)

## Summary

Wildfire is the underground resistance and mutual-aid network founded by the physician Natasha to sustain Underground Belobog after the surface city's Supreme Guardian left the district to fend for itself. It becomes the Trailblazer's operational base during the Jarilo-VI arc, drawing in allies like Seele, Sampo Koski, and eventually Clara and Svarog, and its quiet resistance is ultimately vindicated once Cocolia's manipulation is exposed and Bronya reunites the surface and Underground. The group is a grassroots survival and resistance movement, not affiliated with any Path or Aeon.

## Overview

An underground resistance and mutual-aid movement formed by Underground Belobog's citizens, smuggling medicine and supplies to a population the surface city's Supreme Guardian has left to fend for itself.

## Origin

Wildfire formed in response to Underground Belobog's isolation after the mining operations that once sustained the district were cut off, severing the district's residents from surface resources and, functionally, from surface society altogether. Led by the physician **[Natasha](../../characters/detail/natasha.md)**, the organization operates as both a practical survival network and a quiet, ongoing act of political resistance against the Supreme Guardian's neglect.

## Members

- **[Natasha](../../characters/detail/natasha.md)** — founder and leader; the Underground's primary physician.
- **[Seele](../../characters/detail/seele.md)** — a prominent fighter within the organization, shaped by the same scarcity Wildfire exists to address.
- **[Sampo Koski](../../characters/detail/sampo.md)** — a loosely affiliated smuggler and con artist whose unreliable reputation belies real usefulness to the cause.
- **[Clara](../../characters/detail/clara.md) and [Svarog](../../characters/detail/svarog.md)** — become allied with the Underground community more broadly during the arc, gathering vagrants around Svarog's base under Clara's quiet leadership.

## Role in the story

Wildfire becomes the Trailblazer's operational base once the crew is framed and forced to flee into the Underground, and its network of allies (Seele, Sampo, eventually Clara and Svarog) forms the core of the party's support structure through the second half of the Jarilo-VI arc. The organization's eventual vindication — once Cocolia's manipulation is exposed and Bronya begins reuniting the surface and Underground — represents one of the story's clearest "underdog resistance proven right" arcs.
