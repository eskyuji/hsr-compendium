# The Ten-Lords Commission and the Realm-Keeping Commission (Detail)

## Summary

The Ten-Lords Commission and Realm-Keeping Commission are two of the Xianzhou Luofu's governing bodies, operating alongside (but distinct from) the Alliance's six main flagship commissions. The Ten-Lords Commission specializes in mara corruption, capital crimes, and matters of life and death — including judgment of souls at the Hall of Karma — through four divisions staffed by judges such as Xueyi and Hanya. The Realm-Keeping Commission serves as the Luofu's everyday civic administration and primary law-enforcement/public-service body, handling fraud, riot control, and emergency response. Together they give the Luofu a layered apparatus covering both ordinary law enforcement and the extraordinary threats unique to an immortal, mara-vulnerable society.

## Ten-Lords Commission

Operates independently from the Xianzhou Alliance's six main flagship commissions, specifically handling matters involving **mara corruption** and the Alliance's long-lived native species — a mandate that includes managing those who succumb to mara, prosecuting serious criminals, and overseeing matters of life and death more broadly (including access to the **Hall of Karma**, where souls await judgment).

The Commission is organized into four divisions — **Detention, Incarceration, Interrogation, and Punishment** — staffed by judges, spiritfarers, jailers, secretaries, and scribes (with scribes sometimes outnumbering spiritfarers due to the sheer volume of record-keeping the Commission's work generates).

Known judges:
- **[Xueyi](../../characters/detail/xueyi.md)** — Detention Division; a combat-oriented judge who personally hunts criminals and oversees prisoner transfers using a mechanical ingenium body after her original form was destroyed by mara corruption.
- **Hanya** — Interrogation Division; a non-combat judge who interprets karmic records and gathers intelligence to help prevent disasters before they occur.

## Realm-Keeping Commission

The Xianzhou's general administrative organization, responsible for population statistics, resource allocation, and calendar timing, while also functioning as the flagship's primary law-enforcement and public-service body — the commission "the public interacts with most," per in-world description. Its remit covers fraud investigation, riot control, criminal investigation, and emergency response, alongside more mundane civic functions like weather management (through its weather pavilion). It maintains a branch office at the Exalting Sanctum on the Luofu and regularly coordinates with the [Alchemy Commission](alchemy-and-artisanship-commissions.md) and the [Divination Commission](divination-commission.md) ("Seat of Divine Foresight") when a case crosses jurisdictional lines.

## Relationship between the two

Where the Ten-Lords Commission handles the Xianzhou's most severe, mara-and-mortality-adjacent cases, the Realm-Keeping Commission handles the broader day-to-day business of civic order and administration — together giving the Luofu's government a fairly complete (if bureaucratically layered) apparatus for both ordinary law enforcement and the extraordinary threats unique to an immortal society.
