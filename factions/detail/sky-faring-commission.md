# The Sky-Faring Commission (Detail)

## Summary

The Sky-Faring Commission is one of the Xianzhou Luofu's ten governing commissions, responsible for airspace management, interstellar navigation, and starskiff maintenance that keeps the Luofu operational and on-course. It is led by Helm Master Yukong and operates largely as background civic infrastructure rather than a driver of the main Stellaron-crisis plotline. No Path/Aeon or faction-allegiance errors were found in this entry; content is accurate but sparse, and the Commission is not affiliated with any Aeon.

## Overview

Manages internal airspace, interstellar navigation, and starskiff maintenance for the Xianzhou Alliance — the institution responsible for keeping a flagship the size of a small world actually moving and navigable.

## Leadership

On the Luofu, the Commission is led by Helm Master **Yukong**, who oversees the practical, logistical side of keeping the immortal flagship operational and correctly charted through the wider cosmos.

## Role in the story

The Sky-Faring Commission operates mostly in the background of the main Luofu arc's plot, providing the practical infrastructure (navigation, airspace management) that the more dramatic institutions — the [Cloud Knights](cloud-knights.md), the [Divination Commission](divination-commission.md), the [Alchemy Commission](alchemy-and-artisanship-commissions.md) — depend on to function. Its comparatively low narrative profile reflects its role as essential but unglamorous civic infrastructure, a contrast to the more overtly dramatic commissions embroiled directly in the Stellaron crisis.
