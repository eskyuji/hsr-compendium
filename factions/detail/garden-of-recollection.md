# The Garden of Recollection (Detail)

## Summary

The Garden of Recollection is a faction devoted to Fuli, the Aeon of Remembrance, whose Memokeepers shed physical form to travel between worlds collecting memories. Its signature technology is the Memory Bubble, and it is also credited as the origin of Light Cones — memory-derived tech significant enough that the IPC keeps it under heavy restriction. Internal divisions like the Cremators (including Constance) and the Memosnatchers, plus the faction's history with Black Swan, Boothill, and the Trailblazer, round out the entry's core content.

## Overview

A faction devoted to **Fuli**, the Aeon of Remembrance (see [`characters/detail/aeons-reference.md`](../../characters/detail/aeons-reference.md)), operating on the core belief that "to think is to exist, and memories are proofs of that" — physical matter eventually perishes, but memory allows things to persist eternally through Remembrance.

## The Memokeepers

Members, called Memokeepers, undergo a transformation into memetic entities, shedding their physical bodies to travel freely between worlds. They collect memories across the universe by any means necessary — trading, copying, stealing, or outright deception — operating under strict internal rules against misusing memory-based powers, with violations reportedly punishable by termination.

## Technology

The Garden's signature memory-preservation technology is the **Memory Bubble**, a simple portable container wrapped in a sentient membrane, used to store and safeguard memories (as seen in [Boothill](../../characters/detail/boothill.md)'s backstory, where Cremators destroyed bubbles containing his memories).

**Light Cones** are also Garden of Recollection technology: created from refined memory fragments, they can store not only memories but also experiences and abilities. Because of this power, they are kept under heavy restriction by the [Interastral Peace Corporation](../interastral-peace-corporation.md).

## Notable internal divisions

- **The Cremators** — a splinter group holding that not all memories deserve preservation, and who selectively destroy memories they judge unimportant. [Constance](../../characters/detail/constance.md), who helps erase and later helps reconstruct the Trailblazer's Penacony-era memories, is a Cremator.
- **The Memosnatchers** — the radicals of the Garden of Recollection, exempted from the faction's standard rules and operating with considerably more autonomy (and correspondingly less oversight) than ordinary Memokeepers.

## Known members and story role

**[Black Swan](../../characters/detail/black-swan.md)** is the faction's best-known figure within the arcs covered by this compendium. After learning of the Trailblazer's existence, the Garden of Recollection took an interest in them specifically because of their ability to hold a Stellaron, and was curious to see how they would influence the universe. The faction's history also includes past friction with Boothill over destroyed Memory Bubbles, and involvement with a Shadow of IX — an unintended creation of IX, the Aeon of Nihility — that was sealed away inside a Memory Bubble within a Memory Zone before later being destroyed by Arbitrators.
