# The Alchemy and Artisanship Commissions (Detail)

## Summary

The Alchemy Commission and Artisanship Commission are two of the Xianzhou Luofu's Ten Lords/Realm-Keeping Commissions, handling medicinal/technological development and craftsmanship respectively. The Alchemy Commission's Elixir Crucible becomes the flashpoint of the Luofu's Stellaron crisis when the Disciples of Sanctus Medicus — a cult that had infiltrated the Commission's own ranks, including Chief Alchemist Dan Shu himself, and secretly served the Ravager Phantylia — weaponize it to spread corrupting Mara "smoke," meaning the disaster originates from betrayal within the institution rather than purely external sabotage. Bailu holds the formal position of Physician of the Luofu within the Alchemy Commission, known to residents as the "Healer Lady." The Artisanship Commission handles the Luofu's craftsmanship and forge work; its lore ties to Heliobi (the Flint Emperor imprisoned as a power source at the Xianzhou Zhuming's Flamewheel Forge) belong to a separate, older storyline and are not established to be connected to damage from the Luofu's Stellaron crisis.

## Alchemy Commission

Supports the Xianzhou Luofu's technological and medicinal development. Its central facility, the **Elixir Crucible** (part of Evemist Mansion), becomes the primary weapon of the Luofu arc's Stellaron crisis: the Disciples of Sanctus Medicus, a cult that had infiltrated the Alchemy Commission's own ranks — with investigations revealing that many Commission members, up to and including Chief Alchemist Dan Shu, had ties to or had joined the sect — and secretly served the Ravager [Phantylia](../../characters/detail/tingyun-phantylia.md), weaponize it to spread the hallucinogenic, corrupting Mara "smoke" that turns exposed citizens and Cloud Knights hostile. **[Bailu](../../characters/detail/bailu.md)** holds the formal position of Physician of the Luofu within the Alchemy Commission, known as the "Healer Lady," bringing genuine compassion to an institution that, elsewhere in the same arc, is betrayed from within by its own cultist staff and turned into an instrument of mass corruption.

## Artisanship Commission

Handles craftsmanship and furnace work for the Xianzhou Alliance, with its most prominent branch — including the prestigious Flamewheel Forge — located on the Xianzhou Zhuming rather than the Luofu. Its lore involves **Heliobi**, an ancient, shapeless energy-based species that first sought refuge with the Xianzhou during the Lone Voyage Era; most notably, the "Flint Emperor," progenitor of the heliobi, was imprisoned after the ancient Heliobi Wars to serve as an inexhaustible power source for the Zhuming forges. This backstory is distinct from the Luofu's Stellaron crisis, and no source establishes that Artisanship Commission facilities were damaged during that crisis or that such damage released Heliobi. Separately, on the Luofu, ghost- and spirit-hunting side content (such as material involving **Huohuo**, a Ten-Lords Commission Judge-in-training who has a heliobus sealed within her by Ten-Lords Commission judges, and **[Sushang](../../characters/detail/sushang.md)**) exists in the game but is tied to the Ten-Lords Commission's judicial role in policing demons and heliobi generally, not to Artisanship Commission crisis damage. Huohuo was introduced in patch 1.3 and released as a playable character in patch 1.5.
