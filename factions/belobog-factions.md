# Factions of Jarilo-VI / Belobog (Index)

## Summary

Belobog's central conflict on Jarilo-VI is institutional and three-sided: the Silvermane Guards, the surface's military and police force nominally loyal to the Supreme Guardian; Wildfire, the Underground's resistance and mutual-aid network led by Natasha; and the Antimatter Legion, an ancient, non-political robotic threat left over from a catastrophe that predates both factions. This document is an index only — full write-ups live in the linked detail files. Faction allegiances here were checked against known lore and no errors were found (this document makes no Aeon/Path allegiance claims, so it does not repeat the Stellaron Hunters/Nanook-style misattribution found elsewhere in the compendium).

Belobog's central conflict is institutional: a surface city with real military and administrative power, an underground population left to fend for itself, and an ancient external threat that predates both. Full write-ups for each faction below live in `factions/detail/`.

- **Silvermane Guards** — Belobog's surface military and police force, nominally loyal to the Supreme Guardian. Notable members: [Gepard Landau](../characters/detail/gepard.md), [Serval](../characters/detail/serval.md), [Pela](../characters/detail/pela.md), [Bronya](../characters/detail/bronya.md) (before she becomes Supreme Guardian). → [`factions/detail/silvermane-guards.md`](detail/silvermane-guards.md)
- **Wildfire** — the Underground's resistance and mutual-aid network, led by [Natasha](../characters/detail/natasha.md), smuggling supplies to citizens the surface has neglected. Notable members: [Seele](../characters/detail/seele.md), [Sampo](../characters/detail/sampo.md). → [`factions/detail/wildfire.md`](detail/wildfire.md)
- **Antimatter Legion** — the destructive, non-negotiable robotic/mechanical force left over from Jarilo-VI's ancient catastrophe; a recurring existential threat rather than a political actor. → [`factions/detail/antimatter-legion.md`](detail/antimatter-legion.md)

## Related, non-factional entries

- **Svarog** — the ancient guardian robot allied with Clara; not a faction in his own right, but central to the Underground's story and Belobog's eventual leverage against IPC debt collection. See [`characters/detail/svarog.md`](../characters/detail/svarog.md).
- **The Underground / Underworld** — not a formal faction, but the whole underclass population sealed beneath Belobog's surface; their struggle for recognition is the arc's emotional core and is covered throughout [`arcs/01-jarilo-vi-belobog.md`](../arcs/01-jarilo-vi-belobog.md) and [`arcs/detail/01-jarilo-vi-belobog.md`](../arcs/detail/01-jarilo-vi-belobog.md).
