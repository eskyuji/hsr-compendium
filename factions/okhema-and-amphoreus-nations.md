# The Nations of Amphoreus (Index)

## Summary

This index doc catalogs Amphoreus's four major city-states (Okhema, Castrum Kremnos, Janusopolis, Northern Empire), each tied to a Chrysos Heir and a patron Titan. Research confirmed most leadership/patron pairings are accurate (Aglaea/Romance at Okhema, Nikador/Strife at Castrum Kremnos with Mydei as its last prince, Janus/Tribbie at Janusopolis, Cerydra/Law at the Northern Empire), but corrected two errors: (1) Okhema's protector Titan is Kephale (Worldbearing), not Aquila (Sky) — official wiki sources confirm Kephale slumbers above Okhema holding the Dawn Device that protects the city from the night, while Aquila's Coreflame belongs to Hyacine/Hyacinthia and is not tied to Okhema's founding or defenses; (2) the claim that Hysilens commands the Northern Empire's military under Cerydra is unsupported — wiki sources identify Hysilens as "Helektra, Daughter of the Sea," the Chrysos Heir who wields the Coreflame of the Ocean and serves as Knight Commander of the coastal city of Styxia, a separate city with no documented military role in the Northern Empire. That claim has been removed pending a verified source.

Amphoreus's political geography is fractured across several city-states, each led by a Chrysos Heir and shaped by the Titan whose domain the city was originally built around. Full write-ups live in `factions/detail/`.

- **Okhema** — "the Eternal Holy City," the last major bastion of civilization, led by [Aglaea](../characters/detail/aglaea.md) and protected by Kephale, the Titan of Worldbearing. The arc's primary hub. → [`factions/detail/okhema.md`](detail/okhema.md)
- **Castrum Kremnos** — a militant city-state, formerly ruled by Nikador (Strife/"the Mad King"); its last prince, [Mydei](../characters/detail/mydei.md), abandoned it after Nikador's fall. → [`factions/detail/castrum-kremnos.md`](detail/castrum-kremnos.md)
- **Janusopolis** — a city of divination and fate, devoted to Janus and led by high priest [Tribbie](../characters/detail/tribbie.md). → [`factions/detail/janusopolis.md`](detail/janusopolis.md)
- **Northern Empire** — an authoritarian state ruled by Imperator [Cerydra](../characters/detail/cerydra.md); Cerydra's climactic self-sacrifice is the turning point of the entire Amphoreus arc. (Note: an earlier draft credited Hysilens with commanding the Empire's military — this could not be verified and appears incorrect, as Hysilens/Helektra is instead tied to the coastal city of Styxia and the Ocean Coreflame; the Empire's military command is currently undocumented.) → [`factions/detail/northern-empire.md`](detail/northern-empire.md)
